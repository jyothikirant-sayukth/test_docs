## <u><b>Door Locked</b></u>
> A Door Locked Record is created when the owner is not available during the survey, and only limited details are collected. Once the owner is available, the full property details can be added and updated.

{{< mediacms url="https://media.panchayatseva.in/embed?m=8lwipjBnU" title="Panchayat Seva Video" >}}

{{< mediacms url="https://media.panchayatseva.in/embed?m=eVVXdcur8" title="Panchayat Seva Video" >}}

### Navigation

1. Open the <b>Survey App</b>.
1. Once opened and enter your Username and Password, then click on the <b>Login</b> button.
1. Once you log in, you will be taken to the Dashboard screen and click on the <b>Menu</b> at the top left corner.<br>
  {{< image src="images/House_images/dashboard_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="580" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. Select the <b>Survey</b> from the <b>Menu</b>.<br>
{{< image src="images/navigation_images/survey_mb.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}
1. After clicking on the <b>Survey</b>, you will be moved to the Survey screen, then look for the <b>Door Locked</b> and click on it.<br>
   {{< image src="images/Door Locked_images/dl_icon.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}

### List
From the Survey screen, click on the <b>  Door Locked</b>, and it moves to the List screen. The List screen displays different sections i.e., Title, Statistics, Records.<br>
   {{< image src="images/list_screens/dl_list_screen.png" alt="Alt text" width="350" height="500" class="img-center">}}


#### Record Section
In the Record Section of the List screen, you can see a list of all created records.
   1. The list of created records are categorized by color codes based on their status, which are displayed on the right side of each record.
      > <b><span style="color: #0000FF;">Blue</span> </b>: The record is created but Not Uploaded.<br>
      {{< image src="images/Door Locked_images/dl_blue.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>
      > <b><span style="color: green;">Green</span></b>: The record is created and Successfully Uploaded.<br>
      {{< image src="images/Door Locked_images/dl_green.png" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}} <br>
      > <b><span style="color: #f4511e;">Red</span></b> : The record is created but Failed to Upload.<br>
         {{< image src="images/Door Locked_images/dl_red.png" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>
      > <b><span style="color: #8B4513;">Brown</span></b>: The record is created but Uploaded through Backup.<br>
         {{< image src="images/Door Locked_images/dl_brown.png" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}
   1. If the List Screen is empty, a message will be displayed as <b>" There are no records available, Tap Add (+) button to add a new record "</b>.<br>

### Create
1. On the List screen, click on the Add <b>"+"</b> button which is located at the bottom right corner and then it moves to the Create screen.<br>
   {{< image src="images/Door Locked_images/dl_create_screen.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}<br>

#### Input Section
{{< notice "note" >}}
You must fill all the Mandatory fields (<b><span style="color: red;">*</span></b>) on the Create screen.{{< /notice >}}
1. Click on the <b>Camera</b> section and use your Device Camera to take a photo of the property. (Mandatory Field)
1. Select the <b>Property Type</b> of the Door Locked from the dropdown. (Mandatory Field)<br>
   {{< image src="images/Door Locked_images/dl_category.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}
   1. <b>Advertisement : </b> If you choose Advertisement, you will see the following Mandatory Fields :<br>
         + <b>Advertisement Name :</b> Enter the Name of the Advertisement.<br>
            + If you click the Info Icon  of the Advertisement property Name, a dialogue box will be displays a message as <b>"Do you want to Auto Generate Property Name?"</b> and then click <b>OK</b> button on the dialogue box to Auto Generate the Property Name in the Confirmation screen.  
         + <b>Category :</b> Choose the Category of the Advertisement from the dropdown.
   1. <b>Panchayat Asset Auction :</b> If you choose Auction, you will see the following Mandatory Fields :<br>
         + <b>Auction Name :</b> Enter the Name of the Auction.<br>
         + <b>Category :</b> Choose the Category of the Auction from the dropdown.<br>
   1. <b>House :</b> If you choose House, you will see the following Fields :<br>
      + <b>House/Apartment :</b> Select the House type.<br>
      + <b>Door Number :</b> Enter the Door number.<br>
          + If you click the <b>Auto Generate</b> button of the Door Number, an dialogue box will be displays a message as <b>"Do you want to Auto Generate Door Number? "</b> and then click <b>OK</b> button on the dialogue box to Auto Generate the Door Number.<br>
      + <b>Block :</b> Choose the Block Number from the dropdown.<br>
      +  Other Fields: Building Category, Land Survey Number and Land Record Type.<br>
   1. <b>Katarusum :</b> If you choose Katarusum, you will see the following Mandatory Fields :<br>
         + <b>Katarusum Name :</b> Enter the Name of the Katarusum.<br>
            + If you click the Info Icon  of the Katarusum Property Name, a dialogue box will be displays a message as <b>"Do you want to Auto Generate Property Name?"</b> and then click <b>OK</b> button on the dialogue box to Auto Generate the Property Name in the Confirmation screen.<br>
      + <b>Category :</b> Choose the Category of the Katarusum from the dropdown.
   1. <b>Trade License :</b> If you choose Trade License, you will see the following Mandatory Fields :<br>
         + <b>Trade License Name :</b> Enter the Name of the Trade License.<br>
            + If you click the Info Icon  of the Trade License Name, an dialogue box will be displays a message as <b>"Do you want to Auto Generate Property Name?"</b> and then click <b>OK</b> button on the dialogue box to Auto Generate the Property Name in the Confirmation screen.<br>
      + <b>Category :</b> Choose the Category of the Trade License from the dropdown.
   1. <b>Vacant Land :</b> If you choose Vacant Land, you will see the following Mandatory Fields :<br>
         + <b>Block :</b> Choose the Block Number from the dropdown.<br>
         + <b>Land Category :</b> Choose the Category of the Vacant Land from the dropdown.
             + <b>Concession :</b> If Concession is chosen, an additional field appears to select a Land Subcategory from Ex-service man, Ex-service man widow, Service Men, or Others.<br>
             + <b>Exemption :</b> If Exemption is chosen, select the relevant Land Subcategory.<br>
    + If you enter the same <b>Name</b> again, a warning alert box will display then click the <b>OK</b> button on the warning alert box to edit the Name.<br>.   
   {{< image src= "images/Door Locked_images/dl_duplicate.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. Choose the <b>Street Name</b> where the property is located (Mandatory Field).<br>
   >+ The Street Name should only start with numbers or alphabets, but not with special characters.<br>
   >+ Allowed Characters <b>_ - / @ # & . A-Z a-z 0-9</b><br>
   >+ Minimum Length <b>3</b><br>
   >+ Maximum Length <b>64</b><br>
   1. If the Street Name is not available in the dropdown, select <b>Others</b> option at the bottom of the dropdown to enter the Street Name in the provided text field.<br>
   1. The newly added Street Name will be included in the dropdown for the next records.<br>
   1. If you enter a Street Name in the <b>Others</b> text field that is already listed in the dropdown, a message will be displayed at the bottom of the screen as <b>"Street Name already exist"</b>.
1. To Capture the Location, click on the <b> Capture the Geo Location</b> section  (Mandatory Field).<br>
   1. For detailed procedure, please click [here](/basic-details/capturing_geo_location/).
1. Once all the required fields are filled, click on the <b>Next</b> button which is at the bottom of the screen, then it moves to the Confirmation screen.<br>
   1. If you click on the <b>Next</b> button without entering all mandatory fields then indformation dialogue box will display.<br>
   {{< image src="images/Kolagaram_images/kol_required_fields.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}<br>
    {{< notice "note" >}}
Even after the uploaded Door Locked record, it can move from Door Locked to the specific property type and become available for upload under that property.
   {{< /notice >}}

###  Confirmation
On the Create screen, click on the <b>Next</b> button and it moves to the Confirmation screen.<br>
   {{< image src="images/Door Locked_images/dl_confirm_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}<br>

#### Details Section
1. Verify all the details displayed in the Confirmation screen.
1. To view your Door Locked Location, click on the <b>Map Pin</b> which is on top right corner in the Confirmation screen.<br>
1. Click on the <b>Finish</b> button at the bottom of the screen and it moves to the List screen.<br>
1. In the List screen, it displays the newly created Door Locked Records.

###  View and Edit 
On the List screen, clicking on a record will move you to the View screen.<br>

#### Details Section
1. Review the details in the View screen.
1. If changes are needed, click on the <b>Edit</b> to make the necessary changes.
1. Click on the <b>Next</b> button and it moves to the Confirmation screen.
1. Editing can only be done before the Door Locked Record is uploaded.
1. Click on the <b>Finish</b> button to save the changes.
1. To Unlock a Door Locked when the owner is available:
1. Click on the <b>Unlock</b> to open the its Respective Property screen for full data entry. Once completed, the Record will appear in its Respective property List screen and it will not visible under Door Locked List screen.
