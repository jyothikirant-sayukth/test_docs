# <u><b>Trade License</b></u>
 >A Trade License provides permission to start a particular Business.

{{< mediacms url="https://media.panchayatseva.in/embed?m=D3k5wP6yE" title="Panchayat Seva Video" >}}

### Navigation
1. Enter your Username and Password, then click on the <b>Login</b> button.
1. Once you log in, you will be taken to the Dashboard screen and click on the <b>Menu</b> at the top left corner.<br>
  {{< image src="images/House_images/dashboard_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="580" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. Select the <b>Survey</b> from the <b>Menu</b>.<br>
{{< image src="images/navigation_images/survey_mb.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}
1. After clicking on the <b>Survey</b>, you will be moved to the Survey screen, then look for the <b>Trade License</b> and click on it.<br>
   {{< image src="images/Trade_License_images/tl_icon.jpg"alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}} 
### List

From the Survey screen, click on the <b>Trade License</b>, and it moves to the List screen. The List screen displays different sections i.e., Title, Statistics, Records.

{{< image src="images/list_screens/tl_list_screen.png"alt="Alt text" width="300" height="450" class="img-center">}}

#### Record Section
In the Record Section of the List screen, you can see a list of all created records.
1. The list of created records are categorized by color codes based on their status, which are displayed on the right side of each record.
   > <b><span style="color: #0000FF;">Blue</span></b> : The record is created but Not Uploaded.<br>
   {{< image src="images/Trade_License_images/tl_blue.jpg"alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>
   > <b><span style="color: #228B22;">Green</span></b> : The record is created and Successfully Uploaded.<br>
   {{< image src="images/Trade_License_images/tl_green.jpg"alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>
   > <b><span style="color: #f4511e;">Red</span></b> : The record is created but Failed to Upload.<br>
   {{< image src="images/Trade_License_images/tl_red.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>
   > <b><span style="color: #8B4513;">Brown</span></b> : The record is created but Uploaded through Backup.<br>
   {{< image src="images/Trade_License_images/tl_brown.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. If the List screen is empty, a message will be displayed as <b>" There are no records available, Tap Add (+) button to add a new record "</b>.<br>

### Create
 On the List screen, click on the Add <b>"+"</b> button which is located at the bottom right corner and then it moves to Create screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/Trade_License_images/tl_create.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}

#### Input Section

{{< notice "note">}} 
You must fill all the Mandatory fields(<b><span style="color: Red;">*</span></b>) on the Create screen.{{< /notice >}}<br>
1. Click on the <b>Camera</b> section and use your Device Camera to take a photo of the Trade License (Mandatory Field).
   1. If you click on the <b>Next</b> button without taking the photo of the property then a message displays at the bottom of the screen as <b> "Please Capture the Property Photo using Camera"</b>.
1. Enter the <b>Trade License Name </b> (Mandatory Field).<br>
   >+ Trade License Name should only start with alphabets and numbers but not with special characters.<br>
   >+ Trade License Name is unique (Duplicate Name is not allowed).<br>
   >+ Allowed Characters <b>_ - / @ # & . A-Z a-z 0-9</b><br>
   >+ Minimum Length <b>3</b><br>
   >+ Maximum Length <b>64</b><br>
      + If a Trade License Property doesn’t have a name, click the <b>Auto Generate</b> button to create one automatically.<br>
      + If you click the Auto Generate button of Trade License Name, an information dialogue box will display, then click <b>OK</b> button on the dialogue box to Auto Generate the Property Name in the Confirmation screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/Trade_License_images/tl_auto_generate_name.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="350" class="img-center">}}
      + If you enter the same "Trade License Name", a warning alert box will display then click on the <b>OK</b> button on the alert box to Edit the Trade License Name.<br>  
      {{< image src="images/Trade_License_images/tl_dup_record.jpg"alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>    
1. Choose the <b> Sector Type</b> from the dropdown (Mandatory Field).
{{< notice "info" >}}If the Sector Type is NGO or Private, you must provide the owner details manually. If the Sector Type is Public, default owner details will be created automatically.{{< /notice >}}<br>
1. Choose the <b> Category</b> from the dropdown (Mandatory Field).
{{< notice "tip" >}} Please select the Sector Type before choosing a Category. {{< /notice >}}<br>
   1. Click on the <b>Search Bar</b> to search the Trade License Category.<br>
   {{< image src="images/Trade_License_images/tl_category.jpeg"alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>
   2. If you don't select any the Category then a message will be displayed at the bottom of the screen as <b>" Please Select Category "</b>.<br>
      {{< notice "info" >}} Government Educational institutions, Government Hospitals  and Government Offices are part of trade but do not have to pay tax.{{< /notice >}}
1. Choose the <b>Street Name</b> from the dropdown where the Trade License property is located (Mandatory Field).<br>
   >+ The Street Name should only start with numbers or alphabets, but not with special characters.<br>
   >+ Allowed Characters <b>_ - / @ # & . A-Z a-z 0-9</b><br>
   >+ Minimum Length <b>3</b><br>
   >+ Maximum Length <b>64</b><br> 
     1. If the Street Name is not available in the dropdown, select <b>Others</b> option at the bottom of the dropdown to enter the Street Name in the provided text field.<br>
     1. The newly added Street Name will be included in the dropdown for the next records.<br>
     1. If you enter a street name in the <b>Others</b> text field that is already listed in the dropdown, a message will be displayed at the bottom of the screen as <b>"Street Name already exists"</b>.
1. Enter the <b>GP Sanction Id</b> (Optionsl).<br>
   >+ GP Sanction Id should only start with numbers or alphabets, but not with special characters.<br>
   >+ GP Sanction Id is unique (Duplicate Id is not allowed).<br>
   >+ Allowed Characters <b>_ - / @ # & . ( ) A-Z a-z 0-9</b><br>
   >+ Minimum Length <b>3</b><br>
   >+ Maximum Length <b>32</b><br>
1. Enter the <b>Annual Turnover (Rs)</b> of the Trade License (Mandatory Field).<br>
{{< notice "info" >}}
 The Annual Turnover will be displayed in Words at the bottom of the field.
{{< /notice >}}
   >+ Allowed Characters  <b>0-9</b><br>
   >+ Minimum Length  <b>3</b><br>
   >+ Maximum Length  <b>9</b>
1. Choose the <b>Motor Capacity (in HP)</b> value from the dropdown (Mandatory Field).
   1. If you won't select any option then a message will be displayed at the bottom of the screen as <b>"Please Select Motor Capacity"</b>.<br>    
   {{< notice "note" >}}  
   If the Trade License does not use a motor, select <b>"0"</b> from the dropdown.{{< /notice >}}<br>
1. To Capture the Location, click on the <b> Capture the Geo Location</b> section (Mandatory Field).<br>
   1. For detailed procedure, please click [here](/basic-details/capturing_geo_location/).
1. Enter Owner Details.
   1. To capture Owner Details, please click [here](/basic-details/aadhaar_details/scanner.en.md/).
   1. You can add Multiple Owners by clicking on <b> " + Add Owner " </b>, if needed.  
1. Once all the required fields are filled, click on the <b>Next</b> button which is at the bottom of the screen, then it moves to the Confirmation screen.<br>
1. If you Click on the <b>Next</b> button without entering all mandatory fields then information dialogue box will display.<br>
   {{< image src="images/Kolagaram_images/kol_required_fields.jpg"alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>
   
###  Confirmation 
On the Create screen, click on the <b>Next</b> button and it moves to the Confirmation screen.<br>
   {{< image src="images/Trade_License_images/tl_confirm_screen.jpeg"alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>

#### Details Section
1. Verify all the details displayed on the Confirmation screen.
1. To view your  Trade License Location, click on the <b>Map Pin</b> in the Confirmation screen.<br>
1. Click on the <b>Finish</b> button at the bottom of the screen it moves to the List screen.<br>
1. In the List screen, it displays the newly created Trade License Records.
###  View and Edit 
On the List screen, clicking on a record will move you to the View screen.<br>

#### Details Section
{{< notice "note" >}} Editing can only be done before the Trade License Record is uploaded. {{< /notice >}}<br>
1. Review the details in the View screen.
1. If changes are needed, click on the <b>Edit</b> to make the necessary changes.
1. Click on the <b>Next</b> button and it moves to the Confirmation screen.
1. Click on the <b>Finish</b> button to save the changes.
