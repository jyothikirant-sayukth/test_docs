# <u><b>Panchayat Asset Auction</b></u>
>An Panchayat Asset Auction refers to a formal process by which the Panchayat manages certain assets, and services offered for lease to the highest bidder.

{{< mediacms url="https://media.panchayatseva.in/embed?m=MMuLy7hbm" title="Panchayat Seva Video" >}}

### Navigation
1. Enter your Username and Password, then click on the <b>Login</b> button.
1. Once you log in, you will be taken to the Dashboard screen and click on the <b>Menu</b> at the top left corner.<br>
  {{< image src="images/House_images/dashboard_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="580" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. Select the <b>Survey</b> from the <b>Menu</b>.<br>
{{< image src="images/navigation_images/survey_mb.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}
1. After clicking on the <b>Survey</b>, you will be moved to the Survey screen, then look for the <b>Panchayat Asset Auction</b> and click on it.<br>
{{< image src="images/auction_images/au_icon.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="450" class="img-center">}}

### Create
On the List screen, click on the Add <b> " + " </b> button which is located at the bottom right corner and then it moves to the Create screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/auction_images/au_create_page.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="900" class="img-center">}}<br>
   
#### Input Section
### Auction Details
{{< notice "note">}}
You must fill all the Mandatory fields(<b><span style="color: Red;">*</span></b>) on the Create screen.{{< /notice >}} <br>
1. Click on the <b>Camera</b> section and use your Device Camera to take a photo of the Auction Property (Mandatory Field).<br>
   1. If you click on the <b>Next</b> button without taking the photo of the property then a warning alert box be displayed as <b>"Please Capture the Property Photo using Camera"</b>.<br>
1. Enter the <b>Auction Name</b> (Mandatory Field).<br>
   >+ The Auction Name should only start with numbers and alphabets, but not with special characters.<br>
   >+ Auction Name is unique (Duplicate Name is not allowed).<br>
   >+ Allowed Characters <b>_ - / @ # & . A-Z a-z 0-9</b><br>
   >+ Minimum Length <b>3</b><br>
   >+ Maximum Length <b>64</b><br>
+  If you enter the same "Auction Name" then a warning alert box will be displayed then click the <b>OK</b> button on the warning alert box to edit the Panchayat Asset Auction Name.<br>
    {{< image src="images/auction_images/au_duplicate_box.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}
1. Choose the<b> Category </b> from the dropdown (Mandatory Field).<br>
   1. Click on the <b>Search Bar</b> to search the Auction Category.<br>
{{< image src="images/auction_images/au_category.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>
   1. If you don't select any option then a message will be displayed at the bottom of the screen as <b>" Please Select Auction Category "</b>.<br>
1. Choose the <b>Street Name</b> from the dropdown where the Auction property is located (Mandatory Field).<br>
   >+ The Street Name should only start with numbers and alphabets, but not with special characters.<br>
   >+ Allowed Characters <b>_ - / @ # & . A-Z a-z 0-9</b><br>
   >+ Minimum Length <b>3</b><br>
   >+ Maximum Length <b>64</b><br>
    1. If the Street Name is not available in the dropdown, select <b>Others</b> option at the bottom of the dropdown to enter the Street Name in the provided text field.<br>
    1. The newly added Street Name will be included in the dropdown for the next records.<br>
    1. If you enter a Street Name in the <b>Others</b> text field that is already listed in the dropdown, a message will be displayed at the bottom of the screen as <b>"Street Name already exists"</b>.
1. Enter the <b>Start Bid (Rs)</b> (Mandatory Field).<br>
   + <b>Start Bid</b> is the Amount announced for an item at the beginning of the Auction.
   >+ Start Bid should only start with numbers but not with alphabets, special characters.
   >+ Allowed Characters <b>0-9</b><br>
   >+ Minimum Length <b>3</b><br>
   >+ Maximum Length <b>8</b><br>
{{< notice "note">}} Start Bid should be less than the End Bid.{{< /notice >}}<br>
1. Enter the <b>Deposit Amount</b> (Mandatory Field).
   + <b>Deposit Amount</b> is a payment made by the Bidder before the Auction begins.
   >+ Deposit Amount should only start with numbers but not with alphabets, special characters.
   >+ Allowed Characters <b>0-9</b><br>
   >+ Minimum Length <b>3</b><br>
   >+ Maximum Length <b>8</b><br>   
    {{< notice "note" >}} </b> The Deposit Amount should be less or equal to the Start Bid. It can start with 0 but not less than 0. {{< /notice >}}
1. Choose the <b>Tenure Months</b> of the Auction amount from the dropdown (Mandatory Field).<br>
   + The <b>Tenure Months</b> refers to the time period in which the Current winner has owned the Auctioned Property.
1. Choose the <b>Number of Installments</b> of the Auction from the dropdown (Mandatory Field).
   + <b>Installment Months</b> refers to the Number of Months that the winning bidder has to pay for an Auctioned item in monthly payments instead of paying the amount at once.<br>
1. To Capture the Location, click on the <b> Capture the Geo Location</b> section (Mandatory Field).<br>
   1. For detailed procedure, please click [here](/basic-details/capturing_geo_location/).

### Active Auction Details
1. Enter the <b>End Bid (Rs) </b> value (Mandatory Field).
   + <b>End Bid</b> refers to the final bid amount placed at the end of the Auction, determining the winning price.
   >+ Allowed Characters  <b>0-9</b>
   >+ Minimum Length  <b>3</b>
   >+ Maximum Length  <b>8</b><br>
   <b>Note:</b> End Bid should be always greater than or equal to Start Bid.<br>
1. The <b>Due Amount </b> and <b>Installment Amount </b> will display automatically when End Bid is Entred.
1. Select the <b>Auction Date</b> from the calendar (Mandatory Field).
   + The Auction Date is the day when the Auction happens, and people can place bids to buy items.
   >+ The Auction Date calendar will display dates up to 5 years before the current date.<br>
1. Select the <b>Tax Start Date</b> from the calendar (Mandatory Field).
   + The <b>Tax Start Date</b> refers to the beginning date for tax collection.
   >+ The Tax Start Date calendar will show dates up to 5 years from the current date.<br>
   >+ The Tax End Date calendar will display the dates up to 5 years from the Current Date.
1. Enter the <b>Tender Number</b> of the Auction.
   + The <b>Tender Number</b> refers to a Unique Identification Number given to an item being Auctioned.
   >+ The Tender Number should only start with numbers and alphabets, not special characters.<br>
   >+ Allowed Characters  <b>_ - / : , & A-Z a-z 0-9</b><br>
   >+ Minimum Length  <b>3</b><br>
   >+ Maximum Length  <b>64</b><br>
1. Enter Winner Details.
   1. For Further Winner Details, please click [here](/basic-details/aadhaar_details/scanner.en.md/).
   1. You can add Multiple Winners by clicking on <b>"+ Add Winner"</b> , if needed.<br>
1. Once all the required fields are filled, click the <b>Next</b> button which is at the bottom   of the screen, then it moves to the Confirmation screen.<br>
   1. If you click the <b>Next</b> button without entering all mandatory fields then information dialogue box will be displayed.<br>
   {{< image src="images/auction_images/au_req_fields.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}<br>

###  Confirmation
On the Create screen, click the <b>Next</b> button and it moves to the Confirmation screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/auction_images/au_confirm_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="300" height="800" class="img-center">}}

#### Details Section
1. Verify all the details displayed on the Confirmation screen.
1. To view your Auction Location, click on the <b>Map Pin</b> which is in the top right corner on the Confirmation screen.<br>
. Click the <b>Finish</b> button at the bottom of the screen and it moves to the List screen.<br>
1. The List screen displays the newly created Auction records.
###  View and Edit 
On the List screen, clicking on a record will move you to the View screen.<br>

#### Details Section
1. Review the details in the View screen.
1. If changes are needed, click on the <b>Edit</b> to make the necessary changes.
1. Click on the <b>Next</b> button and it moves to the Confirmation screen.
1. Editing can only be done before the Auction Record is uploaded.
1. Click on the <b>Finish</b> button to save the changes.
