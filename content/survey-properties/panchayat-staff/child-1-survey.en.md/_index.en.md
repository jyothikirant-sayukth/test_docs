# <u><b>Panchayat Staff</b></u>
> Panchayat Staff are the members who manage the services in a Panchayat.

{{< mediacms url="https://media.panchayatseva.in/embed?m=vlTxNxFqG" title="Panchayat Seva Video" >}}

### Navigation
1. Enter your Username and Password, then click on the <b>Login</b> button.
1. Once you log in, you will be taken to the Dashboard screen and click on the <b>Menu</b> at the top left corner.<br>
  {{< image src="images/House_images/dashboard_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="580" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. Select the <b>Survey</b> from the <b>Menu</b>.<br>
{{< image src="images/navigation_images/survey_mb.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}
1. After clicking on the <b>Survey</b>, you will be moved to the Survey screen, then look for the <b>Panchayat Staff</b> and click on it.<br>
  {{< image src="images/PanchayatStaff_images/ps_icon.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>

### List
From the Survey screen, click on the <b>Panchayat Staff</b> and it moves to the List screen. The List screen displays different sections i.e., Title, Statistics, Records.<br>
  {{< image src="images/list_screens/ps_list_screen.png" alt="Alt text" width="300" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>

#### Records Section
In the Record Section of the List screen, you can see a list of all created records.
 1. The list of created records are categorized by color codes based on their status, which are displayed on the right side of each record.
    > <b><span style="color: #0000FF;">Blue</span></b> : The record is created but Not Uploaded.<br>
    {{< image src="images/PanchayatStaff_images/ps_blue.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>
    > <b><span style="color: #228B22;">Green</span></b> : The record is created and Successfully Uploaded.<br>
    {{< image src="images/PanchayatStaff_images/ps_green.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>
    > <b><span style="color: #f4511e;">Red</span></b> : The record is created but Failed to upload.<br>
    {{< image src="images/PanchayatStaff_images/ps_red.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>
    > <b><span style="color: #8B4513;">Brown</span></b> : The record is created but Uploaded through Backup.<br>
    {{< image src="images/PanchayatStaff_images/ps_brown.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. If the List screen is empty, a message will be displayed as <b>" There are no records available, Tap Add (+) button to add a new record "</b>.<br>       
### Create

On the List screen, click on the Add <b>" + "</b> button which is located at the bottom right corner to move to the Create screen.<br>
    {{< image src="images/PanchayatStaff_images/ps_create.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="300" height="650" class="img-center">}}<br>

#### Input Section
{{< notice "note">}}
You must fill all the Mandatory fields (<b><span style="color: Red;">*</span></b>) on the Create screen.<br>
{{< /notice >}} <br>

1. Click on the <b>Camera</b> section and use your Device Camera to take a photo of the Panchayat Staff(Mandatory Field). <br>
    1. If you click on the <b>Next</b> without taking the photo of the Panchayat Staff then a message displays at the bottom of the screen as   <b> "Please Capture the Panchayat Staff Photo using Camera"</b>.<br>
1. Enter the Staff Details.
    1. To capture Staff Details, Click [here](/basic-details/aadhaar_details/scanner.en.md/).
   + If you enter the same "Staff Details", a warning alert box will display then click on the <b>OK</b> button on the alert box to Edit the Staff Details.<br>  
 {{< image src="images/PanchayatStaff_images/ps_duplicate_record.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. Enter the <b>Email ID</b>. 
    > + Allowed Characters <b> _ - + </b>
    > + Minimum Length <b> 6 </b>
    > + You must follow a Standard Email format (e.g. Example123@gmail.com) 
    > + If the entered Email is invalid, an  dialogue box will display as <b>"Please enter a valid Email address."</b>
1. Choose the <b>Education Qualification</b> from the dropdown. (Mandatory Field) <br>
    {{< image src="images/PanchayatStaff_images/ps_category.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>
   1. By default, <b>Choose</b> option is selected in the dropdown, If you won't select the Education Qualification then a message will be displayed at the bottom of the screen as <b>" Please Select Education Qualification"</b>.<br>
1. Choose the <b>Designation</b> from the dropdown. (Mandatory Field) <br>
   1. Click on the <b>Search Bar</b> to search the Designation.<br>
   {{< image src="images/PanchayatStaff_images/ps_designation_category.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>
   1. By default, <b>Choose</b> option is selected in the dropdown, If you won't select the Designation then a message will be displayed at the bottom of the screen as <b>" Please Select Designation "</b>.<br>
1. Select the <b>Appointment Date</b> from the calendar. (Mandatory Field) <br>
    >  + Calender displays only past dates<br>
    >  + The appointed date should be considered the joining date in that panchayat.
    >  + If you did not Select <b>Appointment Date</b> from the Calender, a message will be displayed at the bottom of the screen as <b>"Please Select Appointment Date"</b><br>
1.  Once all the required fields are filled, click on the <b>Next</b> button which is at the bottom of the screen, then it moves to the Confirmation screen.<br>
  1. If you click on the <b>Next</b> without entering all mandatory fields then information dialogue box will display.<br>
    {{< image src="images/PanchayatStaff_images/ps_req_fields.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}} <br>

### Confirmation Screen
On the Create screen, click on the <b>Next</b> it moves to the Confirmation screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/PanchayatStaff_images/ps_confirm_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}} <br>
 
#### Details Section
1. Verify all the details displayed in the Confirmation screen.
1. Click on the <b>Finish</b> button at the bottom of the screen and it moves to the List screen.<br>
1. In the List screen, it displays the newly created Panchayat Staff records.

###  View and Edit 
On the List screen, clicking on a record will move you to the View screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/PanchayatStaff_images/ps_view_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}} <br>

#### Details Section
1. Review the details in the View screen.
1. If changes are needed, click on the <b>Edit</b> to make the necessary changes.
1. Click on the <b>Next</b> button and it moves to the Confirmation screen.
1. Editing can only be done before the Panchayat Staff Record is uploaded.
1. Click on the <b>Finish</b> button to save the changes.
