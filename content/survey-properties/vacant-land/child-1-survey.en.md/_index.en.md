# <u><b>Vacant Land</b></u>
> Vacant Land means land that has no buildings and it is not being used.

{{< mediacms url="https://media.panchayatseva.in/embed?m=JdKt7Nl4k" title="Panchayat Seva Video" >}}

###  Navigation

1. Enter your Username and Password, then click on the <b>Login</b> button.
1. Once you log in, you will be taken to the Dashboard screen and click on the <b>Menu</b> at the top left corner.<br>
  {{< image src="images/House_images/dashboard_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="580" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. Select the <b>Survey</b> from the <b>Menu</b>.<br>
{{< image src="images/navigation_images/survey_mb.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}
1. After clicking on the <b>Survey</b>, you will be moved to the Survey Screen, then look for the <b>Vacant Land</b> and click on it.<br>
{{< image src="images/VacantLand_images/vl_icon.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}

##  List
From the Survey Screen, click on the <b>Vacant Land</b> and it moves to the List Screen.
The List screen displays different sections i.e., Title, Statistics, Records.<br>
{{< image src="images/list_screens/vl_list_screen.png" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}<br>

#### Record Section

In the Record Section of the List Screen, you can see a list of all created records.
   1. The list of created records are categorized by color codes based on their status, which are displayed on the right side of each record.
      > <b><span style="color: #0000FF;">Blue</span></b> : The record is created but Not Uploaded.<br>
      {{< image src="images/VacantLand_images/vacant_land_blue.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>
      > <b><span style="color: #228B22;">Green</span></b> : The record is created and Successfully Uploaded.<br>
      {{< image src="images/VacantLand_images/VacantLand_green.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>
      > <b><span style="color: #f4511e;">Red</span></b> : The record is created but Failed to Upload.<br>
      {{< image src="images/VacantLand_images/vl_red.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>
      > <b><span style="color: #8B4513;">Brown</span></b> : The record is created but Uploaded through Backup.<br>
      {{< image src="images/VacantLand_images/VacantLand_brown.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. If the List Screen is empty, a message will be displayed as <b>" There are no records available, Tap the Add (+) button to add a new record "</b>.
### Create
On the List Screen, click on the Add <b> " + " </b> button which is located at the bottom right corner and then it moves to the Create Screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/VacantLand_images/vl_create_page.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>

   #### Input Section

{{< notice "note">}}
You must fill all the Mandatory fields(<b><span style="color: Red;">*</span></b>) on the Create Screen.<br>{{< /notice >}}<br>

1. Click on the <b>Camera</b> section and use your Device Camera to take a photo of the Vacant Land (Mandatory Field).<br>
   1. If you click on the <b>Next</b> button without taking the photo of the property then a message will displays as <b>"Please Capture the Property Photo using Camera"</b>.<br>
2. Choose the <b>Land Category</b> from the dropdown (Mandatory Field).<br>
   {{< image src="images/VacantLand_images/vl_category.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="220" height="350" class="img-center">}}<br>
   1. <b>Concession : </b> The lands in which the tax has a subsidy collected from retired servicemen.<br>
   2. <b>Exemption : </b> The Vacant lands related to the panchayat where tax is not applicable.
   3. <b>Panchayat : </b> These are parts of land that are not currently being used for any specific purpose such as agriculture, construction. These lands will be owned by the Panchayat.
   4. <b>Private : </b> Private lands are the lands that are owned by individuals, companies, or non-governmental organizations, rather than by the government or the public.
3. Choose the <b>Land Sub Category</b> from the dropdown (Mandatory Field).<br>
{{< notice " note ">}} The Land Sub Category will be displayed only when you choose <b>Concession or  Exemption or Private</b>.{{< /notice >}}
1. Choose the <b>Street Name</b> from the dropdown where the Vacant Land property is located (Mandatory Field).<br>
   >+ The Street Name should only start with numbers and alphabets, but not with special characters.<br>
   >+ Allowed Characters <b>_ - / @ # & . A-Z a-z 0-9</b><br>
   >+ Minimum Length <b>3</b><br>
   >+ Maximum Length <b>64</b><br>
   1. If the Street Name is not available in the dropdown, select <b>Others</b> option at the bottom of the dropdown to enter the Street Name in the provided text field.<br>
   2. The newly added Street Name will be included in the dropdown for the next records.<br>
   3. If you enter a Street Name in the <b>Others</b> text field that is already listed in the dropdown, a message will be displayed at the bottom of the screen as <b>"Street Name already exists"</b>.
2. Choose the <b>Block</b> from the dropdown (Mandatory Field).<br>
3. Choose the <b>Land Measurement Type</b> from the dropdown (Mandatory Field).<br>
   1. Site Area, Site Area(in Sq.Yards) fields will be displayed when you select Cents, Gunthas, Square Feet, Length * Breadth.
   2. Site Area(in Sq.Yards) field will be displayed when you select Yards.
4. Enter the <b>Site Area</b> value for the selected Land Measurement Type.
5. If you enter the <b>Site Area</b> value, then the <b>Site Area(in Sq.Yards)</b> value can be generated automatically.
6. To Capture the Location, Click on the <b> Capture the Geo Location</b> section  (Mandatory Field).<br>
   1. For detailed procedure, please click [here](/basic-details/capturing_geo_location/).
7. Enter Owner Details.
   1. To capture Owner Details, please click [here](/basic-details/aadhaar_details/scanner.en.md/).
8. Select <b>Is Owner Alive</b> of the Owner.
   1. If you Select <b>" Yes "</b>, enter the Owner Details.
   2. If you Select <b>" No " </b>, <b>Aadhaar Card Available</b> field will be displayed.
      1. Select <b>Aadhaar Card Available</b> of the Owner.
         1. If you Select <b>" Yes "</b>, enter the Owner Details.
         2. If you Select <b>" No "</b>, Aadhaar Number will be generated automatically.
   3. You can add Multiple Owners by clicking on <b> " + Add Owner " </b> , if needed.
{{< notice "note">}}The Owner Details section will not be displayed for the <b>Panchayat</b> and <b>Exemption</b> Category.{{< /notice >}}
1. Once all the required fields are filled, click on the <b>Next</b> button which is at the bottom of the screen, then it moves to the Confirmation screen.<br>   
      1. If you Click on the <b>Next</b> button without entering all mandatory fields then information dialgue box will display.<br>
      {{< image src="images/VacantLand_images/vl_req_fields.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="230" height="400" class="img-center">}}

###  Confirmation
On the Create screen, click on the <b>Next</b> button and it moves to the Confirmation screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/VacantLand_images/vacantland_confirm_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>

#### Details Section
1. Verify all the details displayed on the Confirmation screen.
1. To view your Vacant Land Location, click on the <b>Map Pin</b> which is on the top right corner on the Confirmation Screen.<br>
1. Click the <b>Finish</b> button at the bottom of the screen and it moves to the List screen.<br>
1. The List screen displays the newly created Vacant Land records.
###  View and Edit 
On the List screen, clicking on a record will move you to the View screen.<br>

#### Details Section
1. Review the details in the View screen.
1. If changes are needed, click on the <b>Edit</b> to make the necessary changes.
1. Click on the <b>Next</b> button and it moves to the Confirmation screen.
1. Editing can only be done before the Vacant Land Record is uploaded.
1. Click on the <b>Finish</b> button to save the changes.
