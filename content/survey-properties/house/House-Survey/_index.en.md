# <u>House</u>
>House in a panchayat is a building that people used to reside regularly.

##  Navigation

1. Enter your Username and Password, then click on the <b>Login</b> button.
1. Once you log i n, you will be taken to the Dashboard screen and click on the <b>Menu</b> at the top left corner.<br>
  {{< image src="images/House_images/dashboard_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="600" class="img-center">}}<br>

## Create a House
> House includes the collection of House Details, Building, Owners, Household Details.

{{< notice "note" >}}
You must fill all the Mandatory fields (<b><span style="color: Red;">*</span></b>) on the create screen. {{< /notice >}}

In the Create a House section, you will find a list of options that provide to detailed information of the House. Here are the details :
* House Details
* Buildings
* Owners
* Households
* Survey Pending

#### House Details Section
1. House Details includes the collection of Building Category, Land Details & Amenities.
1. To enter the house details, click on <b>" + "</b> button then it moves to the <b>House Details</b> screen.<br>`
{{< image src="images/House_images/house_details_create.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="260" height="570" class="img-center">}}

##### Input Section
{{< notice "note">}}You must fill all the Mandatory fields(<b><span style="color: Red;">*</span></b>) on the Create screen.{{< /notice >}}<br>
1. Click on the <b>Camera</b> section and use your Device Camera to take a photo of the House.
   1. If you click <b>Next</b> without taking the photo of the property then an alert message will display as  <b> " Please Capture the Property Photo using Camera "</b>.
1. Choose the <b>Building category</b> from the dropdown.<br>
    1. <b>Exemption</b>: The Houses related to the panchayat where tax is not applicable.<br>
       {{< notice "note" >}}
Households and Owner details will not be available for Exemption Category.
{{< /notice >}}<br>
    1. <b>Commercial</b>: A Building that used for business activities, including retail, offices, hotels, and other revenue generating operations.<br>
      {{< notice "note" >}}Households details will not be available for Commercial Category.<br>
{{< /notice >}}<br>
    1. <b>Concession</b>: The land in which the tax has a subsidy collected from servicemen.<br>
    2. <b>Residential</b>: A building in which people to live in.
1. Choose <b>House/Apartment</b> from the dropdown (Mandatory Field).<br>
{{< notice "tip" >}} If you choose Exemption in the Building Category, the House option is selected automatically.
{{< /notice >}}<br>
   1. If you select <b>House</b>, you have to enter the Door Number.
    1. If you select <b>Apartment</b>, additional fields will display : 
       1. Apartment Name
       1. Community Name
{{< notice "info" >}} 
Each flat we consider it as a house.<br>
If you want to add other flat details, you can click on the Add <b> " + "</b> button on the List screen.<br>
The Apartment site area must be less than 5000 square yards.{{< /notice >}}<br>
1. Enter the <b>Door Number</b> of the House. 
   >+ Minimum Length <b>1</b>
   >+ Maximum Length <b>64</b>
   >+ The Door Number should start with Alphabet or Number, but not with special characters.
   >+ Allowed characters <b>_ - / @ # & . : () , A-Z a-z 0-9</b> <br>
     > + If a house doesn’t have a door number, click the <b>Auto Generate</b> button to create one automatically.<br>
     > + If you click the Auto Generate button of the Door Number, a dialogue box will display, then click <b>OK</b> button on the dialogue box to Auto Generate the Door Number.<br>
    {{< image src="images/House_images/h_door_no_ag.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="350" class="img-center">}}<br>
    <b>Note:</b>  Enter the Door Number for the Apartment as <b> Door Number/Flat Number</b>.
1. Enter the <b>Svamitva Document Number</b> (Optional).
   >+ The Svamitva Document Number should only start with alphabets.
   >+ Allowed Characters <b>- / @ # & _ ( , ). A-Z a-z 0-9</b><br>
   >+ Minimum Length <b>3</b>
   >+ Maximum Length <b>14</b>
1. Choose the <b>Street Name</b> from the dropdown where the House property is located (Mandatory Field).<br>
   >+ The Street Name should only start with numbers and alphabets, but not with special characters.<br>
   >+ Allowed Characters <b>_ - / @ # & . A-Z a-z 0-9</b><br>
   >+ Minimum Length <b>3</b><br>
   >+ Maximum Length <b>64</b><br>
    1. If the Street Name is not available in the dropdown, select <b>Others</b> option at the bottom of the dropdown to enter the Street Name in the provided text field.<br>
    1. The newly added Street Name will be included in the dropdown for the next records.<br>
    1. If you enter a Street Name in the <b>Others</b> text field that is already listed in the dropdown, a message will be displayed at the bottom of the screen as <b>"Street Name already exists"</b>.
1. Choose the <b>Block Number</b> of the House (Mandatory Field).<br>

#### Land Details
{{< image src="images/House_images/h_land_Details_form.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="290" height="440" class="img-center">}}
1. Select the type of <b>Land Measurement</b> from the dropdown.<br>
{{< image src="images/House_images/House_MeasurmentType.png" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>
  1. Depending on your Measurement Type, additional fields will appear for entering the House Measurements.
    {{< notice "note" >}}If you select Apartment and the plinth area is available, then enter it.Otherwise, take the plinth area from the sale deal.<br>
{{< /notice >}}<br>
1. The Land Area will be generated automatically in Sqare yards (sq. yd) based on the House Measurements entry.
1. Select the <b>Land Survey Number</b> assigned to the property.
1. Choose the <b>Land Record Type</b> assigned to the property.<br>
{{< image src="images/House_images/house_land_record_type.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="450" class="img-center">}}
1. Choose <b>Legal issues</b> status associated with that property.
   1. Select <b>" Yes "</b> button, if the property has legal issues (i.e., it is a Legal Land).
   1. Select <b>" No "</b> button, if the property has any illegal issues (i.e., it is an illegal Land).
#### Amenities
{{< image src="images/House_images/h_amenities_form.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="270" height="480" class="img-center">}}
1. Select the <b>Water Tap Connections</b> count from the dropdown.
1. Select the <b>Toilet Count</b> from the dropdown.
1. Select the <b>Soak Pits</b> count from the dropdown.
1. Select the Number of <b>Trees Count</b> from the dropdown.
1. Choose the appropriate <b>Drainage Type</b> from the dropdown.<br>
{{< image src="images/House_images/house_drain_type.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. Choose the appropriate <b>Road Type</b> from the dropdown.<br>
{{< image src="images/House_images/house_road_type.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. To Capture the Location, click on the <b> Capture the Geo Location</b> section (Mandatory Field).<br>
   1. For detailed explaination, please click [here](/basic-details/capturing_geo_location/).
1. Once all the required fields are filled, click on the <b>Next</b> button which is at the bottom of the screen, then it moves to the Confirmation screen.<br>   
   1. If you click on the <b>Next</b> button without entering all mandatory fields then information dialogue box will display, then click <b>OK</b> button on the dialogue box.<br>
{{< image src="images/House_images/house_req_fields.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>
#### Confirmation 
On the House Details screen, click on the <b>Next</b> button, it moves to the House Details Confirmation screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/House_images/house_details_confirm.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="600" class="img-center">}}<br>

#####  Details Section
1. Verify all the details displayed on the Confirmation screen.
1. After Verifying, click on the <b>Save</b> button at the bottom of the screen and it moves to the Create a House Screen.<br>
1. In the Create a House screen, it displays the newly created Records.
## Buildings
1. On the Create a House screen, click on the Add <b> " + "</b> button next to Buildings, it moves to the Buildings screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/House_images/buildings_form.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="350" class="img-center">}}
{{< notice "note" >}} You must create the top sections before creating lower sections.
{{< /notice >}}
 
### Input Section
{{< notice "note">}}
The Land Area will be generated automatically in Square Yards based on the selected measurement type given in the <b>House Details</b>.<br>
{{< /notice >}}
1. You can view the <b>Land Area</b> of the Building which was entered during creation of House Details.
1. Choose the Cellar Floors.
{{< notice "tip">}}
A cellar is a room under a house or building. If the house has no cellars, choose 0 as the number of cellar floors.
{{< /notice >}}
1. Choose the appropriate <b>Roof Type</b> of the Building.<br>
{{< image src="images/House_images/h_r_type.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="350" class="img-center">}}
   1. <b>AC Sheets or Zinc Sheets</b>: Houses built with asbestos cement sheets.
   1. <b>Container</b>: Houses built from unused shipping containers.
   1. <b>Country or Mangalore Tiles</b>: Houses made from fired clay.
   1. <b>RCC</b>: Houses built with cement concrete.
       1. <b>Add Floor</b> button will only be available for RCC, if the House construction is completed.
   1. <b>Shabad Stones</b>: Houses built from natural limestone.
   1. <b>Thatched or Mud Roof</b>: Houses built from dry grass or leaves or clay or mud.
   1. <b>Others</b>: Choose the option <b>Others</b>, if above listed Roof Types do not applies to the building.
1. Select the <b>Construction Status</b> of the House.
 {{< notice "note" >}}  
 <b>Completed</b> - If the House has walls and a slab.<br>
 <b>Not Completed</b> - If the House has pillars or walls but no slab.{{< /notice>}}<br>
1. If the construction of the House is completed, select <b>Completed</b>.
          - If Completed, select <b>Age or Year</b>.  
    1. If the Construction of the House is not completed, select <b>Not Completed</b>.
      {{< notice "note" >}} If the construction year is before 1925, please enter 1925 as the year.
      If the Age is greater than 100, enter 100. If Age is less than 1, choose the year option. {{< /notice>}}<br>
    1. If you select a Year before 1925, a message will appear at the bottom of the screen as <b>" Enter Valid Construction year"</b>.<br>
1. If you choose House, it shows <b>Construction Area</b>.
1. If you choose Apartment, it shows <b>Built-Up(Carpet and share of common)(in Sq.ft.)</b>.
. Enter the Construction Area/Built-Up(Carpet and share of common)(in Sq.ft.).
    1. Ensure that the <b>Construction Area</b> is less than or equal to the Land Area.
{{< notice "note" >}} Please refer to Sale Deed or Record of Rights Documents.{{< /notice >}}
    1. If the Construction Area is greater than the Land Area, an alert box will displays a message as <b>"Total Construction Area should be Less Than or Equal to Land Area"</b>.
    {{< notice "note" >}} Measurement Type value /9 = Land Area {{< /notice >}}
1. Once all the required fields are filled, click on the <b>Next</b> button which is at the bottom of the screen, then it moves to the Confirmation Screen.<br>   
   1. If you click on the <b>Next</b> button without entering all mandatory fields then information dialogue box will display, then click <b>OK</b> button on the dialogue box.<br>
{{< image src="images/House_images/building_req_fields.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}<br>

#### Confirmation Screen
On the Building Details screen, click on the <b>Next</b> button, it moves to the Building Details Confirmation screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/House_images/h_building_details.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="280" height="400" class="img-center">}}

##### Details Section

1. After Verifying, click on the <b>Save</b> button at the bottom of the screen and it moves to the Create a House screen.<br>

1. Head Details includes the Collection of the Family Head Details.
1. On the Household Details screen, click on the Add <b>" + "</b> button next to Head, it moves to the Head Details screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/House_images/h_head_create.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="240" height="650" class="img-center">}}

## Survey Pending
1. At the Bottom of the <b>Create A House</b> screen, you have to select whether the survey for the House is still pending or completed.
      + Click <b>Yes </b> button, If the Survey is still in progress.
      + Click <b>No </b> button, If the Survey is completed.
1. Once you have entered all the required details to create a House and marked the Survey status, then click the <b>Done</b> button, and it moves to the House List screen.
1. On the List screen, it displays the newly created House Records.
#### View and Edit 
On the List screen, Select a House Record to View or Edit.<br>

##### Details Section
1. If changes are needed, click on the <b>Edit</b> which is located on top right corner, it moves to the Create a House screen.
1. Click on the (House/Buildings/Owners/Household) section you want to edit, and it moves to the selected View screen.
1. Review the details in the View screen.
1. Click on the <b>Edit</b>, you will be moved to the Edit screen to modify the changes.
1. Click on the <b>Next</b> button, it moves to the Confirmation screen.
1. Editing can only be done before the House Record is uploaded.
1. Click on the <b>Finish</b> button to save the changes and it moves to the Create a House screen.<br>
