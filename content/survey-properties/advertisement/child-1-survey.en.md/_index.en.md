# <u>Advertisement</u>
>An Advertisement is an announcement to promote about a product, service or an event.

{{< mediacms url="https://media.panchayatseva.in/embed?m=SjQMiElA8" title="Panchayat Seva Video" >}}

###  Navigation

1. Enter your Username and Password, then click on the <b>Login</b> button.
1. Once you log in, you will be taken to the Dashboard screen and click on the <b>Menu</b> at the top left corner.<br>
  {{< image src="images/House_images/dashboard_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="580" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. Select the <b>Survey</b> from the <b>Menu</b>.<br>
{{< image src="images/navigation_images/survey_mb.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}
1. After clicking on the <b>Survey</b>, you will be moved to the Survey screen, then look for the <b>Advertisement</b> and click on it.<br>
{{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_icon.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}

##  List
From the Survey Screen, click on the <b>Advertisement</b> and it moves to the List screen. 
The List screen displays different sections i.e., Title, Statistics, Records.<br>
{{< image src="images/list_screens/ad_list_screen.png" alt="Alt text" width="300" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>

   #### Record Section
 In the Record Section of the List Screen, you can see a list of all created records.
   1. If the List Screen is empty, a message will displays as <b>" There are no records available, Tap the Add (+) button to add a new record "</b>.
   2. The list of created records are categorized by color codes based on their status, which are displayed on the right side of each record.
       > <b><span style="color: #0000FF;">Blue</span></b> : The record is created but Not Uploaded.<br>
   {{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_blue.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>
       > <b><span style="color: #228B22;">Green</span></b> : The record is created and Successfully Uploaded.<br>
   {{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_green.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>
       > <b><span style="color: #f4511e;">Red</span></b> : The record is created but Failed to Upload.<br>
   {{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_red.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>
       > <b><span style="color: #8B4513;">Brown</span></b> : The record is created but Uploaded through Backup.<br>
   {{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_brown.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>

### Create
On the List Screen, click on the Add <b> " + " </b> button which is located at the bottom right corner and then it moves to the Create Screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_create.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>

   #### Input Section
  {{< notice "note">}}You must fill all the Mandatory fields(<b><span style="color: Red;">*</span></b>) on the Create Screen.{{< /notice >}}<br>
1. Click on the <b>Camera</b> section and use your Device Camera to take a photo of the Advertisement Property (Mandatory Field).<br>
   1. If you click on the <b>Next</b> button without taking the photo of the property then a message will displays as <b>"Please Capture the Property Photo using Camera"</b>.<br>
1. Enter the <b>Advertisement Name</b> (Mandatory Field).<br>
    > + The Advertisement Name should only start with numbers and alphabets, but not with special characters.<br>
    > + Advertisement Name is unique (Duplicate Name is not allowed).<br>
    > + Allowed Characters <b>_ - / @ # & . A-Z a-z 0-9</b><br>
    > + Minimum Length <b>3</b><br>
    > + Maximum Length <b>64</b><br>
      + If an advertisement doesn’t have a name, click the <b>Auto Generate</b> button to create one automatically.<br>
      + If you click the Auto Generate button of the Advertisement property Name, information dialogue box will display then click <b>OK</b> button on the warning dialogue box to Auto Generate the Property Name in the Confirmation screen.<br>
    {{< image src="images/advertisement_images/adv_auto_generate_name.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}<br>
      + If you enter the same <b>"Advertisement Name"</b> again, a message will display, then click the <b>OK</b> button on the warning alert box to edit the Advertisement Name.<br>
   {{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_duplicate_record.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. Choose the <b>Street Name</b> from the dropdown where the Advertisement property is located (Mandatory Field).<br>
   >+ The Street Name should only start with numbers and alphabets, but not with special characters.<br>
   >+ Allowed Characters <b>_ - / @ # & . A-Z a-z 0-9</b><br>
   >+ Minimum Length <b>3</b><br>
   >+ Maximum Length <b>64</b><br>
   1. If the Street Name is not available in the dropdown, select <b>Others</b> option at the bottom of the dropdown to enter the Street Name in the provided text field.<br>
    1. The newly added Street Name will be included in the dropdown for the next records.<br>
    1. If you enter a Street Name in the <b>Others</b> text field that is already listed in the dropdown, a message will be displayed at the bottom of the screen as <b>"Street Name already exists"</b>.
1. Enter the <b>Board Size</b> of the Advertisement (Mandatory Field).<br>
   >+  Allowed Characters <b>0-9</b><br>
   >+ Minimum length <b>1</b><br>
   >+ Maximum length <b>5</b><br>
1. Enter the <b>GP Sanction Id</b> (Optional).<br>
   >+ GP Sanction Id should only start with numbers and alphabets, but not with special characters.<br>
   >+ GP Sanction Id is unique (Duplicate Id is not allowed).<br>
   >+ Allowed Characters <b>_ - / @ # & . ( ) A-Z a-z 0-9</b><br>
   >+ Minimum length <b>3</b><br>
   >+ Maximum length <b>32</b><br>  
1. Choose the <b> Advertisement Category </b> from the dropdown (Mandatory Field).<br>
{{< image src="images/advertisement_images/adv_category.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}
1.  Enter the <b>Capital/Insured Amount (Rs)</b> value  of the Advertisement (Mandatory Field).<br>
      >+ Allowed Characters  <b>0-9</b><br>
      >+ Minimum Length  <b>3</b><br>
      >+ Maximum Length  <b>9</b><br>
1. To Capture the Location, click on the <b> Capture the Geo Location</b> section  (Mandatory Field).<br>
   1. For detailed Procedure, please click [here](/basic-details/capturing_geo_location/).
1. Enter Owner Details.
   1. To capture Owner Details, please click [here](/basic-details/aadhaar_details/scanner.en.md/).
   1. You can add Multiple Owners by clicking on <b>" + Add Owner "</b> , if needed.
1. Once all the required fields are filled, click on the <b>Next</b> button which is at the bottom of the screen, then it moves to the Confirmation screen.<br>
   1. If you click on the <b>Next</b> button without entering all mandatory fields then an informaiton dialogue box will display, then click <b>OK</b> on the dailogue box.<br>
   {{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_reqd_fields.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}<br>
###  Confirmation
On the Create screen, click on the <b>Next</b> button and it moves to the confirmation screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_confirm_screen.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>

#### Details Section
1. Verify all the details displayed on the confirmation screen.
1. To view your Advertisement Location, click on the <b>Map Pin</b> which is on top right corner on the Confirmation screen.<br>
1. Click on the <b>Finish</b> button at the bottom of the screen and it moves to the List screen.<br>
1. In the List screen, it displays the newly created Advertisement Records.
###  View and Edit 
On the list screen, clicking on a record will move you to the view screen.<br>

#### Details Section
1. Review the details in the view screen.
1. If changes are needed, click on the <b>Edit</b> to make the necessary changes.
1. Click on the <b>Next</b> button and it moves to the Confirmation screen.
1. Editing can only be done before the Advertisement Record is uploaded.
1. Click on the <b>Finish</b> button to save the changes.
