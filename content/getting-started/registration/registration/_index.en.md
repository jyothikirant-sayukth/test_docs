# <u>Registration</u>
You should register into the Survey App to enroll as a Survey Executive.

{{< mediacms url="https://media.panchayatseva.in/embed?m=uju6mQlGu" title="Panchayat Seva Video" >}}
    
### Navigation
1. From the Login Screen, click on the <b>Register</b> and it moves to the Register screen.
1. The User can follow the registration steps by clicking the <b>Next</b> and <b>Get Started</b> buttons, then it moves to Register screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/register_images/register_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="290" height="1020" class="img-center">}}<br>
#### Input Section
1. Click on the <b>Camera</b> Section to take a photo of the User using the Device Camera for the Profile Picture (Mandatory Field).
1. Enter the User Details.
1. For Further User Details, please click [here](/basic-details/identity-details/).
1. Choose the <b>Marital Status</b> of the User (Mandatory Field).
    1. You cannot enter the <b>Marital Status</b> without entering the age.
1.  Enter the <b>Mobile Number</b> of the Citizen (Mandatory Field).
    >+  Citizen Mobile Number should only start with 6-9.
    >+  Allowed Characters <b>0-9</b><br>
    >+  Length <b>10</b><br>
1. Enter the <b>Email Id</b> of the User (Mandatory Field).<br>  {{< notice "tip">}} This Email Id will be the Username for <b>Login</b> of Survey App after Succesful Registration. {{< /notice >}}<br>
    >+ Allowed Characters <b> ! @ # $ % ^ & + = - A-Z a-z 0-9</b>.
    >+ Minimum Length <b>8</b>.
    >+ You must follow a Standard Email Format (e.g. Example123@gmail.com).
    >+ If the entered Email Id is invalid, a message will be displayed at the bottom of the screen as <b>"Please enter a valid Email Id."</b>
1. Create a <b>Password</b> for your Survey App Account (Mandatory Field).<br> 
    {{< notice "tip" >}}Password must have more than 8 characters with a combination of uppercase, lowercase, numbers & special characters <b>! @ # $ % ^ & + = - </b>.{{< /notice >}}
1. Enter the password in the <b>Confirm password</b> that should matches to the above password.
1. Choose the <b>Survey Company Name</b> from the dropdown.
1. Choose the User's <b>T-Shirt Size</b> from the dropdown.

##### Mobile Details
1. The following <b>Mobile Device details</b> are generated automatically.
    >+ <b>Mobile OS Version :</b>  OS Version of the User's Mobile.
    >+ <b>Mobile Manufacturer :</b> User's Mobile Name.
    >+ <b>Mobile Model :</b> Model of the User's Mobile.
    >+ <b>Mobile Serial Number :</b> Serial Number of the User's Mobile.
<br>
1. Once all the required fields are filled, click on the <b>Next</b> button then it moves to the User Details Screen.

### User Details
On the Register Screen, click on the <b>Next</b> it moves to the User Details Screen.<br>


{{< image src="images/register_images/user_details.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="290" height="1000" class="img-center">}}<br>


#### Details Section
1. Accept the <b>"Terms and Conditions"</b> & <b>"Mobile Network Availability"</b>.
1. Check all the information on the User Details screen, then click the 'Send OTP' button to go to the OTP Verification screen..<br>
### OTP Verification


{{< image src="images/register_images/otp_register_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="300" height="600" class="img-center">}}<br>


   1. Enter the <b>OTP</b> which is sent to the registered user's mobile number and email. Click <b>Confirm</b> button to go to the success screen. Otherwise, remains in the same screen unless you enter the valid OTP.<br>
        {{< notice "tip">}}If you don’t enter the OTP on time, click the <b>Resend</b> button to receive a new one.
{{< /notice >}}
### Success


{{< image src="images/register_images/register_success.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="300" height="550" class="img-center">}}<br>


1. The Screen displays a message as <b>" You have Successfully registered as Survey Executive. Please check your SMS and Email for next steps</b>.
1. Click the <b>OK</b> button to go to the Login Screen. After registration, your Survey Manager will contact you and guide you on the next steps.
1. The next step is to take the <b>Qualification Test</b>, and your login credentials for the Survey App will be the email and password you provided during registration.
{{< notice "info">}}If you have any questions or encounter any issues during registration, please contact your Survey Manager for assistance.
{{< /notice >}}
