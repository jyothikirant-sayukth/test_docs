# <u>Trainify</u>
To Enroll as a Survey Executive, it is mandatory to attempt the exam in the Trainify Portal.

{{< mediacms url="https://media.panchayatseva.in/embed?m=PeG1dPxHa" title="Panchayat Seva Video" >}}

# Navigation
1. Open the <b>Survey App</b> and Register.  
1. After successful [Registration](/getting-started/registration/child-1-registration/) you must take an exam on the <b>Trainify</b> Portal.
1. Log in to the <b>Survey App</b> using the Username and Password you created during registration.  
1. The <b>Survey Manager</b> must approve the Survey Executive to take the exam.  
1. Once approved, click the <b>Login</b> button, it moves to the <b>Take Test</b> screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/trainify_images/test_button.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}
1. Click on the <b>I am ready for the exam</b> button, you will be moved to the <b>Sign In</b> Screen.<br>

### Input Section
{{< notice "info" >}} You must attempt the exam to enroll as a Survey Executive, with a maximum of 10 attempts, failing all 10 times will make you ineligible for the exam. {{< /notice>}} 
1. Enter your <b>Username</b> and click on the <b>Next</b> button.  
{{< image src="images/trainify_images/test_signin.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}
1. Enter the <b>Password and Captcha</b>, then click <b>Sign In</b> button. <br>
{{< image src="images/trainify_images/test_password.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}
1. Then Take Test screen will be opened.
   {{< image src="images/trainify_images/td_testbutton.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}
1. Review the exam instructions and click <b>Take Test</b> to begin.<br>
1. After the exam, click <b>Submit</b> to return to the Test Details Screen.
   1. If you <b>Pass</b>, log in to the Survey App with your credentials.
   1. .If you <b>Fail</b>, click Take Test to retake the exam.
1. You should Logout and Login again to access the <b>AP PS Survey</b> App.<br>
{{< image src="images/trainify_images/test_result.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}

> Once successfully you finish the test, a message will display below saying you’ve completed it. Click the word <b>here</b> which is in the blue colour to log out.

 {{< notice "tip">}}
 If you quit the exam due to any inconvenience, such as a network issue, you can continue the exam from where you left off.<br>
 If you fail the exam, <b>Retake Exam</b> button will display, allowing you to take the exam again.
{{< /notice >}}
