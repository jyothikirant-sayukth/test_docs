# <u> Forgot Password</u>
The <b>Forgot Password</b> feature helps you reset your password if you've forgotten it.

{{< mediacms url="https://media.panchayatseva.in/embed?m=Dboaf1p23" title="Panchayat Seva Video" >}}

## Navigation 
1. Open the <b>Survey App</b>.   
1. If the entered password is incorrect, then an alert box will be displayed as <b>Invalid Password</b> and click on <b>OK</b> button to change your password.
1. Click on the <b>Forgot Password</b> in the Login screen, it moves to the Forgot Password screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/fp_images/click_fp.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="290" height="390" class="img-center">}}

### Input Section
1. Enter the <b>Email Id</b> provided at the time of registration and click <b>Send OTP</b> button.
{{< image src="images/fp_images/enter_username.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="290" height="390" class="img-center">}}
   1. If the entered email is invalid, a warning alert box will displayed as <b>Invalid Username</b>. Click <b>Ok</b> on it to re-enter</b>.
{{< image src="images/fp_images/invalid_username.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="290" height="390" class="img-center">}}
1. Then enter the <b>OTP</b> sent to your registered email.<br>
{{< image src="images/fp_images/otp_enter.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="290" height="390" class="img-center">}}
{{< notice "note" >}} You need to enter the OTP before it expires.{{< /notice>}}
  1. If the OTP expires, <b> Resend OTP</b> button will be displayed. Click on Resend OTP button to resend.
1. Click on <b>Verify OTP</b> button, it moves to the next screen to set a New Password.<br>
1. Enter the <b> New Password</b> and re-enter the password in the <b>Confirm Password</b> field. 
   {{< image src="images/fp_images/new_password.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="290" height="390" class="img-center">}}
{{< notice "note" >}} Password must have more than 8 characters with a combination of uppercase, lowercase, numbers & special characters *!@#$%^&+=.<br>
   Allowed Charecters <b>* ! @ # & . % ^ + = $ A-Z a-z 0-9</b><br>
   Minimum Length <b>8</b><br>
   Maximum Length <b>50</b><br>
{{< /notice>}}
1. Ensure the password given in the new password & confirm password fields are the same.  
1. Click the <b>Change Password</b> button to update your password and a message will display as <b>Your password has been updated successfully</b> then you will move to the login screen.
