# <u>Panchayat Selection</u>
> The Survey Executive needs to select the Panchayat to conduct the Survey.<br>

{{< notice "info">}} The Survey Manager will assign the Panchayat to the Survey Executive.{{< /notice>}}

{{< mediacms url="https://media.panchayatseva.in/embed?m=wnBcm7qPh" title="Panchayat Seva Video" >}}

### Navigation 
1. Open the <b>Menu</b> on the top left corner of the Dashboard Screen.<br>
  {{< image src="images/House_images/dashboard_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="580" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. From the <b>Menu</b>, select the <b>Panchayat</b>.<br>
{{< image src="images/navigation_images/panchayat_mb.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="240" height="450" class="img-center">}}
1. After Clicking on <b>Panchayat</b>, it moves to Panchayat screen.<br>
{{< image src="/images/panchayat_selection_images/panchayat_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}
1. Click on <b> Select Panchayat</b> button, it moves to Panchayat Selection screen.<br>
 {{< image src="/images/panchayat_selection_images/panchayat_selection.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="200" height="350" class="img-center">}}

### Input Section
>+ You cannot select the lower fields without selecting the Upper fields.<br>
For e.g. You cannot select Divison without selecting the district.<br>
>+ If you select the Divison without selecting the District, an alert box will be displayed with a message as <b>"Please Select District"</b>. <br>

{{< image src="images/panchayat_selection_images/district_alert.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="200" height="350" class="img-center">}}<br>

>+ If you Select the Panchayat which is not assigned to you, then an alert box will be displayed with a message as <b>"You have no permission to start the Survey."</b><br>

{{< image src="images/panchayat_selection_images/permission_alert.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="200" height="350" class="img-center">}} <br>
1. Choose the <b>District</b> from the dropdown.
1. Choose the <b>Divison</b> from the dropdown.
1. Choose the <b>Mandal</b> from the dropdown.
1. Choose the <b>Panchayat</b> from the dropdown.
1. Choose the <b>Village</b> from the dropdown.
1. Click on <b>Start Survey</b> button, it moves to the <b>Geo Maps</b> screen.
