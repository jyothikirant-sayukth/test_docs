# <u>Login</u>
Survey Executives will login using their Credentials.

{{< mediacms url="https://media.panchayatseva.in/embed?m=BmhBA1odz" title="Panchayat Seva Video" >}}

## Navigation
Open the AP PS Panchayat Seva app, and the login page will display.<br>
{{< image src="images/Login_images/login_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}

{{< notice "tip" >}}The Candidate who has passed the exam, Registered, and is approved by the Survey manager can only log in to the Survey App.{{< /notice>}}
## Login Process
Once you are on the Login Page, follow these steps to log in:<br>
+ Enter the <b>Username</b> and <b>Password</b> and click on the <b>Login</b> button to access the App.
+ If you enter the wrong credentials the following steps will occur :
1. <b>Invalid Credentials</b> : You will see an Error message indicating the Login failure, such as :
Invalid username/password.<br>
{{< image src="images/Login_images/login_invalid.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="350" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. <b>Forgot Password</b> : If you've forgotten your password, click the Forgot Password to reset. For further details, Click [here](/getting-started/forgot-password/).
1. <b>Account Locked</b>: If you attempt to log in with incorrect credentials multiple times, your account may be temporarily locked.
2. <b>To unlock</b>:If the user is locked out after entering an invalid username or password for multiple times, an alert box will display showing a toll-free number along with the TOTP.
{{< image src="images/Login_images/user_locked.png" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}
+ The survey executive should call the toll-free number and provide the TOTP.
