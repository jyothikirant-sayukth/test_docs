# <u>Archive GIS Maps</u>
If you are unable to download the maps due to low internet connection or no internet, you can use this feature.
{{< notice "note" >}}
  <b>Sender :</b> The person who wants to transfer the GIS Maps to another mobile.<br>
  <b>Receiver :</b> The person who will receive the GIS Maps from sender.
{{< /notice >}}

{{< mediacms url="https://media.panchayatseva.in/embed?m=DEIEmcg6F" title="Panchayat Seva Video" >}}


## Navigation

1. Open the <b>Menu</b> on the top left corner of the Dashboard screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/House_images/dashboard_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="300" height="730" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. From the <b>Side Menu</b>, select the <b>Archive GIS Maps.</b> <br>
{{< image src="images/archive_GIS_Maps/archive_gis_map.jpeg"alt="Alt text" width="260" height="550" class="img-center">}}
1. After clicking on the Archive GIS Maps, you will be moved to the Archive GIS Maps screen.
{{< image src="images/archive_GIS_Maps/archive_gis_maps_screen.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="270" height="1400"           class="img-center">}}<br>
1. You can see a tutorial that shows how to connect both devices using a hotspot and Wi-Fi.

### Details Section
#### Hotspot Connection Setup
1. The Receiver should turn on the Mobile Hotspot.
1. The Sender should turn on the Wi-Fi and connect to the Receiver's Mobile Hotspot to start the Data Backup.<br>
#### Device Connection
1. Once both mobiles are connected, the Sender's and Receiver's screens will show a simple guide on how to back up survey data.<br>
   + <b> Sender :</b> Sender can see the <b>Send Maps</b> button.<br>
   + <b>Receiver :</b> Receiver can see the <b>Receive Maps</b> button.<br>
   {{< image src="images/archive_GIS_Maps/send_buttons.jpg"alt="Alt text" width="550" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>
   {{< notice "note" >}} The <b>Send Maps</b> button will be visible only when the Sender is connected to the Receiver's Hotspot. {{< /notice>}}
1. The Sender should click on the <b>Send Maps</b> button to send the Maps.<br>
1. The Receiver should click on the <b>Receive Maps</b> button to receive the Maps.<br>
#### IP Address Setup Process
1. Initially, the connection status on both the devices will displayed as <b>Not Connected</b>.<br>
{{< image src="images/archive_GIS_Maps/qr_code _screens.jpg"alt="Alt text" width="550" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. The Sender can receive the Receiver's IP Address and Port Number using one of the following methods:
    ##### QR Code Scan
      1. The Sender should scan the QR code from the Receiver's Screen then the IP Address, Port Number will displayed automatically.  
      1. The connection status will be updated to <b>Connected</b> on both the devices.
    ##### Manual Entry
      1. The Sender manually enters the Receiver's IP Address and Port Number displayed on the Receiver's mobile.
      1. Click on the <b>Connect</b> button, then the connection status will be updated to <b>Connected</b> in both the devices.<br>
{{< image src="images/archive_GIS_Maps/connected_status.jpg"alt="Alt text" width="550" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>
   {{< notice "note">}} If you click on the <b>Connect</b> button without receiving the IP Address and Port Number then an message will display at the bottom of the screen as <b>" Please Provide Valid Receiver IP & Port Number "</b>.{{< /notice >}}<br>
#### Initiating Data Transfer
1. Once Sender and Receiver's Mobiles are connected, both the screens will displays as follows :
   + <b>Receiver's Screen : </b>The Receiver's Screen will display the Receiver Status as <b>Ready to Receive</b> and remains on the same screen, waiting to receive the data from the Sender.<br>
   + <b>Sender's Screen : </b> The Sender's Screen will display the <b>Send</b> button and click on it to send the Data.
1. When the sender taps the Send button, the screen moves to the GIS Maps screen. There, it shows the connection status, <b>Disconnect</b> and <b>Send Maps</b> buttons, and a message at the bottom that displays <b>Zipping Completed</b>.<br>

#### Data Transfer
1. The Sender clicks on the Send Maps, then the maps will transfer from sender mobile to receiver mobile.
   {{< image src="images/archive_GIS_Maps/send_button.jpeg"alt="Alt text" width="390" height="490" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. Then the maps will transfer from sender mobile to receiver mobile.
      {{< image src="images/archive_GIS_Maps/gis_maps_inprogress.jpg"alt="Alt text" width="550" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. Upon Receiving the Maps, message display as :
      + Receiver's Mobile : <b>" All files are received"</b>
1. After that receiver has to accept the checkbox and click on <b>Finish</b> button, then it moves to the <b>Survey</b> Screen.
1. Once the Receiver receives the Maps, the Sender and Receiver can disconnect the connection between the two devices.
   1. If the Sender clicks on the <b>Disconnect</b> button, a warning dialogue box will display.
      + Click <b>" Yes "</b> button, to disconnect the connection and moves to the Archive GIS Maps screen.
      + Click <b>" No "</b> button,  to remain the connection active.<br>
   {{< image src="images/archive_GIS_Maps/disconnect.jpeg"alt="Alt text" width="390" height="490" class="img-center">}}<br>
