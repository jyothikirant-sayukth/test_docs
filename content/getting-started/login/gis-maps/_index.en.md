# <u>GIS Maps</u>
GIS Maps are used to Download the Maps regarding the village.


## Navigation 
1. Click on <b>Start Survey</b> button in panchayat selection screen, it moves to the <b>GIS Maps</b> screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/geo_maps/gis_maps_screen.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="370" height="400" class="img-center">}}

## Download Input Section
1. Read the instructions and click on <b>Download</b> button to download the Panchayat Maps.{{< notice "tip">}} You cannot move to the next or previous screen until you download the village maps.{{< /notice >}}<br> 
 {{< image src="/images/geo_maps/maps_downloading.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="300" height="400" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. If you click on the <b>Back</b> button or if you try to go out of the screen when the maps are downloading, then an dialogue box will display and click on <b>OK</b> button to download the Maps.<br>
    {{< image src="/images/geo_maps/inprogress_dialogue_box.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="300" height="400" class="img-center">}} <br>
    1. In case you lose the internet connection, a warning dialog box will appear. Click <b>OK</b> button.
      {{< image src="/images/geo_maps/gis_maps_no_int.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="300" height="400" class="img-center">}}<br>
    1. After clicking the OK button on the dialogue box, a Resume button will appear to continue downloading the maps.
      {{< image src="/images/geo_maps/gis_maps_resume_button.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="370" height="400" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. After downloading the panchayat maps, you will be moved to <b>Survey</b> screen.
    1. If you face any issues while trying to pin the location on the map, update the map.
    1. For Further Member Details, please click [here](/getting-started/settings/).
