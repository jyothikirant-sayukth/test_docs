# <u>Settings</u>
If you want to Change the App Language or Update the Maps, then you can use this Settings feature.

## Navigation
1. Open the <b>Menu</b> on the top left corner of the Dashboard screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/House_images/dashboard_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="580" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. From the Login screen, click on <b>Settings</b>, it moves to the Settings screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/Login_images/settings_page.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="240" height="450" class="img-center">}}
1. Then you will able to see <b>Languages</b> and <b>Update Maps</b>.<br>
{{< image src="images/Login_images/settings_screen.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="300" height="200" class="img-center">}}<br>

### Details Section
### Langauge
1. Click on the <b>Language</b> Dropdown to Select the Language of your choice.
2. Click on the <b>Done</b> button it changes to your selected language.
3. The App will be visible in your selected Language.

### Update Maps
1. Click on the <b>Update Maps</b>, it will re-direct to the GIS Maps Screen to download the maps.
    1. For Further Details, please click [here](/getting-started/login/gis-maps/).
