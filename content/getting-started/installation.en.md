## <u>How to Install </u>?

{{< mediacms url="https://media.panchayatseva.in/embed?m=1dRVHUnAD" title="Panchayat Seva Video" >}}

1. Open the [Playstore](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.sayukth.meradigit&hl=es_419) App in your Android Mobile.
1. Download the <b>"MeraDigit"</b> App and Install.
1. After Installation, Open the MeraDigit App and enter the 6-digit code which is sent by the Survey Manager.<br  >
{{< image src="images/navigation_images/installation.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="300" height="450" class="img-center">}}
1. Look for the <b>AP Panchayat Seva</b> App and click on it.
1. Go to the details screen of the AP Panchayat Seva App and click the Install button to install the app.
1. Open the AP Panchayat Seva App and allow all permissions on the Device Permissions Screen to access the app.<br>
{{< image src="images/register_images/reg_permisions.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="300" height="600" class="img-center">}}<br>
{{< notice "info">}}If any permission is not granted, then the screen will not move to the Login Screen.{{< /notice >}}
1. After allowing all the Permissions and click on the <b>Continue</b> button, then it moves to the Device Compatibility Screen.<br>
{{< notice "info" >}} Our app supports the Device Compatibility feature, which checks overall device compatibility status to ensure whether the device is supported or not.
{{< /notice >}}<br>
{{< image src="images/register_images/compatibility_check.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="300" height="600" class="img-center">}}
1. Read the instructions and click <b>Start Compatibility Check</b> where you will able see the overall compatibility status.<br>
   {{< image src="images/register_images/device_compatibility.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="300" height="600" class="img-center">}}
1. If your phone has all the required features, it will show <b>Your mobile phone is Supported</b>. Otherwise, it will show <b>Your mobile phone is Not Supported</b>.Then, click the <b>Continue</b> button to go to the Login Screen.
