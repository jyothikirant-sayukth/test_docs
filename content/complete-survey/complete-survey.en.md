# <u>Complete Survey</u>
Once you have successfully completed the survey in one panchayat, you can use the <b>Complete Survey</b> feature to start the survey in another panchayat.
{{< notice "note" >}} Survey Manager will assign you the Panchayat.{{< /notice>}}

{{< mediacms uuid="004_upload_survey_data" >}}

## Navigation
1. Click on the <b>Menu</b> at the top left corner of the Dashboard screen.<br>
  {{< image src="images/House_images/dashboard_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="550" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. Select the <b>Panchayat</b> from the <b>Menu</b>.<br>
{{< image src="images/navigation_images/panchayat_mb.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>
### Panchayat 
1. After clicking on the <b>Panchayat</b>, you will be moved to the Panchayat screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/complete_survey_images/exit_panchayat.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="600" class="img-center">}}
#### Details Section 
In this Section, you will able to see the Panchayat Info and Current Survey Count.
#### Panchayat Info 
It will displays the Village, Panchayat, Mandal, Division, District of the Survey conducted area.
#### Current Survey Count 
1. It will displays the individual count of all the properties you have completed till now will be displayed on the screen.<br>
2. Click on the <b>Complete my Survey</b> button to start the Survey in another panchayat. You will be moved to the Complete my Survey screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/complete_survey_images/cs_survey.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="400" class="img-center">}}<br>
### Complete my Survey 
1. If you want to complete the survey, click the <b>OK</b> button to move to the Survey OTP Verification Screen, which will clear your surveyed data from your mobile.
2. Please make sure the surveyed data is uploaded before completing the survey in the current Panchayat.
   1. If the Surveyed data is not uploaded, a message will displayed on the screen along <b>Upload Survey Data</b> button to upload the surveyed data.<br>
{{< image src="/images/complete_survey_images/cs_not_upload.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="450" class="img-center">}}
### Survey OTP Verification 
1. It will display the Panchayat, Mandal, Division, District of the area where you have conducted the survey and Mobile Number and Email Id of the Survey Executive.
2. Survey Executive will receive an OTP through SMS and Email.<br>
{{< image src="/images/complete_survey_images/cs_otp.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>
#### Input Section
{{< notice "note" >}} You need to enter the OTP before it expires.{{< /notice>}}
1. Survey Executive need to enter the OTP that is notified to the Survey Executive's mobile.
   1. If you enter an incorrect OTP, a warning alert box will display then click the <b>OK</b> button to enter a new OTP.
   {{< image src="images/complete_survey_images/cs_invalid_otp.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>
2. After entering the OTP, click on the <b>Confirm</b> button to verify it and the survey will stop in the respective Panchayat, and the screen moves to the Success Screen.
   1. If the OTP expires, <b> Resend OTP</b> button will be displayed and click on it receive a new OTP.

### Success 
{{< image src="/images/complete_survey_images/cs_success.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. You will get an SMS or email with a confirmation.
1. You will see the following messages :
   1. You have successfully completed the Survey for the village <b>Village Name</b>.
   1. Please select another Village or Panchayat to start the survey.
{{< notice "note" >}} Survey Manager will assign you the Panchayat.{{< /notice>}}<br>
   1. You need to contact the Survey Manager to Conduct the survey in another Panchayat.
1. You will see the <b>Go to Dashboard</b> and <b>Select Panchayat</b> buttons.
1. If you click on the <b>Go to Dashboard</b> button, you will moved to Dashboard screen.
1. If you click on the <b>Select Panchayat</b> button, you will moved to Panchayat Selection screen.
