###  Delete 
{{< notice "warning" >}}
Deletion can only be done before the record is uploaded.
{{< /notice >}}
1. From list screen, If you want to delete any particular record, click on the minus <b> " - " </b> sign which is located on the top right corner of the record.
{{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_minus.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="200" class="img-center">}}
1. After clicking on minus <b> " - " </b> sign, then a warning dialogue box will display.</b>
   +  Click <b>" Yes "</b> button, if you want to Delete the Record.
   +  Click <b>" No "</b> button, if you don't want to Delete the record.<br>
