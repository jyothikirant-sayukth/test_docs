# <u>Data Backup Upload</u>
The Receiver should upload the received records by using this feature.

{{< mediacms uuid="036_katarusum" >}}

## Navigation 
1. Open the <b>Menu</b> on the top left corner of the Dashboard screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/House_images/dashboard_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="580" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. From the <b>Side Menu</b>, select the <b>Data Backup Upload</b>.<br>
{{< image src="images/navigation_images/backup_upload_mb.jpeg"alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. After clicking on the Data Backup Upload, you will be moved to the Data Backup Upload screen.

### Details Section

#### Uploading the Received Records
1. The Receiver can Upload only the Received records.
1. The Screen displays the Data Backup List.<br>
{{< image src="images/advertisement_images/databackup_images/ad_backup_upload.jpg"alt="Alt text" width="250" height="580" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. Click on the Advertisement , you will be moved to Advertisement Backup Upload screen.
{{< image src="images/advertisement_images/databackup_images/ad_db_upload_button.jpg"alt="Alt text" width="350" height="300" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. Check the Received Records count and click on the <b>Upload</b> button to Upload.
1. After Uploading, the Received Records count will be reset to <b>0</b>.
