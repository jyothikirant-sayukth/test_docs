# <u>Upload</U>
To upload the records, you should have a Stable Internet Connection. After successfully creating the records, you need to upload them to the Panchayat Seva Cloud Server.

{{< mediacms url="https://media.panchayatseva.in/embed?m=RxDNwI2NU" title="Panchayat Seva Video" >}}

### Navigation
1. Open the <b>Menu</b> on the top left corner of the Dashboard screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/House_images/dashboard_screen.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="580" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. From the <b>Side Menu</b>, select the <b>Upload Survey Data</b>.<br>
{{< image src="images/navigation_images/upload_mb.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. After clicking on the <b>Upload Survey Data</b>, you will be moved to the Upload Survey Data screen.<br>
{{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_upload_screen.jpg"  alt="Alt text" width="350" height="300" class="img-center">}}<br>

#### Details Section

In the property upload section, It updates different colors based on the upload status. For example:<br>
> <b><span style="color: #0000FF;">Blue</span></b> : The record is <b>Created but Not Uploaded</b>.<br>
  {{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_upload_blue.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="350" height="300" class="img-center">}}<br>

> <b><span style="color: #228B22;">Green</span></b> : The record is <b>Created and Successfully Uploaded</b>.<br>
  {{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_upload_green.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="350" height="300" class="img-center">}}<br>
  
> <b><span style="color: #f4511e;">Orange</span></b> : The record is <b>Created but Failed to Upload</b>.<br>
  {{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_upload_red.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="350" height="300" class="img-center">}}<br>
  
#### Upload Process
1. Click on the <b>Upload</b> button to start the upload process.<br>
{{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_upload_button.jpg"alt="Alt text" width="350" height="300" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. You will see a <b>Progress Bar</b> that displays the number of records uploading.<br>
{{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_progress_bar.jpg"alt="Alt text" width="350" height="300" class="img-center">}}<br>
   1. If you go back while the upload is in progress then an information dialogue box will display, then click <b>OK</b> button to complete the upload process.<br>
{{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_progress_pending.jpg"alt="Alt text" width="300" height="400" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. A dialogue box that displays uploaded and total records count and a status dialogue box will display, then click <b>OK</b> button.<br>
{{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_upload_success.jpg"alt="Alt text" width="300" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. After the records are successfully uploaded then the <b>Upload</b> button will be updated by <b>No Data to Upload</b> will be displayed.
1. A dialogue box that displays Uploaded, Failed, and Total Records Count and a message will be displayed as <b>"A few Properties are not uploaded. Please check the property view page for errors "</b> then click on the <b>OK</b> button to edit the records.<br>
{{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_failed_record.jpg"alt="Alt text" width="300" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. If a record fails, you can see why it failed on its view screen.
1. The Upload screen displays the <b>Updated Number of Records</b> that have been Completed or Failed.<br>
{{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_upload_update.jpg"alt="Alt text" width="400" height="300" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. If you click on the <b>Upload</b> button when the records are failed then a warning dialogue box display. <br>
{{< image src="images/advertisement_images/ad_upload_edit.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="300" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. If you see any Failed records in the Upload screen, go to the List screen of Advertisement and see the reason for the failure in the View screen.<br>
    > Flow to view the reason for Failed Record in the View screen :
      >+ Menu bar-->Survey-->Advertisement-->Failed record-->you can see the reason for failed record.
