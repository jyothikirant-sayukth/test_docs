# <u> Geo Location Capturing</u>:
1. Pin the exact point of the Property on the map and click on the <b>Done</b>, then the screen moves back to the Create screen.<br>
      1. If you don't know the current location, click on the three dots <b>(&#8942;)</b> at the top right corner. Three options will appear as : <b>Pin Current Location</b>, <b>Pin Previous Location</b>, and <b>Pin Landmark Location</b>.
         1. <b>Pin Current Location :</b> The Pin will be marked near to the Current Location.
         1. <b>Pin Previous Location :</b> The pin will be placed at the location that was marked in previous record.
         1. <b>Pin Landmark Location :</b> The Pin will be marked at the Landmark Location.<br>
      {{< image src="images/House_images/map_three_dots.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="500" height="500" class="img-center">}}<br>
      1. If you click on <b>Done</b> without pinning the Location of the property, a warning dialogue box will display, then click <b>OK</b> button on the warning dialogue box to pin the Location.<br>
      {{< image src="images/House_images/h_loc_unpin.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>
      1. If you click <b>Back ( ← )</b> on the Map Screen after pinning the Location then a warning dialogue box will display.<br>
         1. Click <b>" Yes "</b> button, you will be moved to the create screen and Location will be captured.<br>
         1. Click <b>" No "</b> button,  you will be moved to the create screen and Location will not be captured.<br> 
          {{< image src="images/House_images/h_loc_warning.jpeg" alt="Alt text" width="250" height="450" class="img-center">}}<br>
1. If you click <b>Done</b> after capturing the Location, the <b>Geo Location</b> section will be updated by the message displaying <b> Geo Location Captured</b>.<br>
