# Device Manager
A Device Manager is responsible for managing device collection, registering, and assigning devices to the panchayats.
## Persona 
1. <b>Qualification :</b> A Graduate with decent knowledge on Electronic Gadgets.
1. Maintaining a positive environment for managing devices by tracking, assigning, and incident management of devices.
1. Capable of handling device Incidents within SLA(Service level Agreement), following to organzational timelines.
1. Should have fluency in English for communication.
1. Should have an understanding of devices and SIM cards, along with basic knowledge of incident handling.
## Responsibilities
1. Responsible for Collecting Device, SIM & updating stock.
1. Responsible for Registering a device and sim into system.
1. Responsible for Allocating devices and preparing them for dispatch to the Panchayat.
1. Responsible for Updating system generated incident with shipment info.
