# Survey Executive

Survey Executives are the employees of Manpower company responsible for conducting the Survey in the panchayat.

## Persona
1. <b> Qualification : </b> He Should be Male, aged 14 or older and have completed 10th grade or higher.
1. Should have limited English fluency.  
1. Requires an Android device running version 10 or higher.  
1. Shows a preference for completing the survey while ensuring accurate information.
1. Should complete the survey according to the given guidelines.
### Responsibilities 

#### Registering as a User
1. Ensure the app is installed on an Android device with version 10 or higher.  
1. Users register themselves in the Panchayat Seva Survey app using their Aadhaar details and personal information.  
#### Going Through Tutorials
1. Watch all training videos and User Manual provided in the Login Screen to understand the survey process.  
1. Learn about categories like houses, vacant land, trade licenses and more.  
#### Attempt the Test
1. Take the mandatory test after completing the tutorials to qualify as a Survey Executive.  
#### Conducting Surveys
1. Begin the Survey process in the assigned panchayat as guided by the Survey Manager.  
1. Collect data accurately from the citizens.
1. Survey Executives should communicate effectively with citizens to ensure the collection of complete and accurate data.
#### Uploading Data Periodically
1. Upload collected data in the Panchayat Seva Cloud Server regularly to avoid data loss.  
1. Ensure Surveyed Data is uploaded before moving to the next panchayat.  
#### Completing the Assigned Panchayat Survey
1. Mark the assigned panchayat as completed in the app once all data is collected and Uploaded.  
#### Moving to the Next Panchayat 
1. Follow the Survey Manager’s instructions to start survey in the next assigned panchayat.
