# Survey Manager
The Survey Manager is a representative of the Manpower Company responsible for managing the Survey process.
{{< notice "note" >}} The Support Team will create the Survey Manager's account.{{< /notice >}}
## Persona
1. <b> Qualification :</b> Should have leadership qualities.
1. Should create a supportive environment to track Survey Team's work.
1. Should have fluency in english and regional languages for effective communication.
1. Should be proficient in selecting Survey Executives according to the criteria.
## Responsibilities
1. Responsible for Company Registration.
1. Responsible for assigning a panchayat to the Survey Executive.
1. Responsible for starting the Survey at Panchayat.
1. Responsible for tracking the Survey executive's work.
## How to Login ?
1. Enter the [sb.panchayatseva.com](https://app.sb.panchayatseva.com/apu/home) in the web browser (Click on the link).
1. Click on the <b>Sign In</b> button located on the top right corner to open the web portal login screen.
1. Support Team will create the Survey Manager's account.
1. Enter the credentials in the [Login Screen](https://app.sb.panchayatseva.com/apu/auth).
   1. At the bottom left corner, you'll find the <b>Contact Support</b> option. 
   1. Clicking on it will take you to the contact screen, where you can reach out to the Support Team for any assistance.
   1. At the bottom right corner, you can find <b>Forgot Password</b> and if you click on the forgot password, it allows you to reset the password.
      1. If you click on the forgot password, it moves to the next screen.
      1. Enter the registered <b>Email Id</b>.
      1. Enter the <b>Captcha</b> displayed on the screen and click on the <b>Verify & Send OTP</b> button.
      1. Enter the <b>SMS OTP and Email OTP</b> which is notified to the registered email id.
      1. Enter the Captcha displayed on screen and click on the <b>Verify OTP</b> button, it moves to the login screen.
      1. A message will be displayed on the screen as <b>Password Successful</b>.
1. Enter the username and click on the <b>Next</b> button, it moves to the next screen.
{{< notice "note" >}} The screen will proceed to the next screen only if the username entered is correct.{{< /notice >}}
1. Enter the Password and Captcha and click on the <b>Sign In</b> button.

### Registering into the Survey Company  
Provide the details of your Survey Company to create your Company profile.
#### Navigation
1. Look for the <b>Asset Survey</b> option in the side menu on the left side of the screen, and then find the <b>Survey Company</b> and by clicking on it will take you to the Survey Company screen.
#### Survey Company
1. <b>Search Icon : </b> It is located at the top right corner and if you click on this Search icon, it allows you to search for the Companies.
##### Create
To Create a Survey Company, click on the <b>Create</b> button and then it moves to the Create Screen.<br>
{{< notice "note">}}
Fill all the Mandatory fields(<b><span style="color: red;">*</span></b>) on the Create Screen. {{< /notice >}}<br>
1. Enter the <b>GST Number</b> and click on the <b>Validate GST</b> button (Mandatory field).
   >+ The GST Number should only start with numbers but not with alphabets.
   >+ Allowed Characters <b>A-Z 0-9</b><br>
   >+ Length <b>15</b><br>

   {{< notice "note">}} If entered GST Number is valid, then the Company details will be automatically generated except Head Count. The Company details cannot be editable.{{< /notice >}}<br>

1. Enter the <b>Head Count</b> of the Company (Mandatory field).
   >+ Allowed Characters <b>0-9</b><br>
   >+ Minimum Length <b>1</b><br>
   >+ Maximum Length <b>4</b><br>
   >+ Range <b>5000</b><br>
1. Check the Company details which are generated.
1. Then, enter the <b>Captcha</b> displayed on the screen (Mandatory field).
1. At the bottom right corner, you can find the <b>Back</b> button and if you click on this it moves back to the Survey Company Screen.
1. Once all required fields are filled, click on the <b>Submit</b> button and then it moves to the Survey Company screen.
   1. After successful creation of the company, a message will be displayed at the top of the screen as <b> " A Survey Manpower Company Successfully Created". </b>
   1. If the Company creation fails, a message will be displayed at the top of the screen as <b> " Failed to Process the Request ".</b>

   1. If the Company updation fails, a message will be displayed at the top of the screen as <b> " Failed to Process the Request ".</b>

### Start/Stop the Panchayat
The Survey Manager should Start/Stop the Survey in a Panchayat.
{{< notice "note" >}} Selecting a Panchayat is mandatory to Start/Stop a Panchayat.{{< /notice >}}<br>
#### Navigation
1. Look for the <b>Product Settings</b> option in the side menu on the left side of the screen, and then find the <b>Organizations</b> and by clicking on it will take you to the Organizations screen.
1. Select the checkbox to view the Organizations details.
   1. If you click on the <b>View</b> button without selecting any checkbox, then an Alert Box is displayed as <b>" Please Select one record to View"</b> and click <b>OK</b> button on the Alert Box to select the checkbox.
1. Click on the <b>View</b> button and then it moves to the View Screen.<br>
{{< notice "note">}} In the View screen, the PDF and Excel buttons allow you to download the details of the Respective Organization.{{< /notice >}}
1. You can view the PDF, Excel, Survey Start buttons and details of the selected Organization.
1. Click on the <b>Survey Start</b> button to start the Survey in the Panchayat and then it moves to the Organization Survey Start screen.<br>
##### Start the Panchayat<br>
1. Survey Manager will assign a Panchayat to the Survey Executive and instruct them to start the Survey.<br>
1. Enter the <b>Target Survey Asset Count</b>.
   >+ Allowed Characters <b>0-9</b>
   > + Minimum Length: <b>1</b>  
   > + Maximum Length: <b>4</b><br>

1. Enter the <b>Reason for Updation</b> (Mandatory Field). 
1. Enter the <b>Captcha</b> displayed on the screen (Mandatory Field).
1. At the bottom right corner, you can find <b>Back</b> button and if you click on this it moves back to the Organizations screen.
1. Once all required fields are filled, click on the <b>Submit</b> button and then it moves to the Organizations screen.
   1. Once the survey is successfully started in a Panchayat, a message will be displayed at the top of the screen as <b>"The Organization has been Successfully Updated".</b>
   1. If the survey launch in a Panchayat fails, a message will be displayed at the top of the screen as <b> " Failed to Process the Request ".</b><br>
##### Stop the Panchayat<br>
1. If the Survey is completed in the Panchayat then the Survey Manager will change the Survey Status Type into <b>Completed</b> then the Survey Manager will stop the panchayat and assign a new panchayat.
1. If the Survey is completed then the Survey Manager will change the Survey Status Type into <b>Completed</b> then the Survey Manager will stop the survey and assign a new panchayat.
1. In order to Stop the Panchayat, go the View Screen and click on the <b>Survey Complete</b> button to stop the Survey in the Panchayat and then it moves to the Organization Survey Complete screen.<br>

1. Enter the <b>Reason for Updation</b>. 
1. Enter the <b>Captcha</b> displayed on the screen.
1. At the bottom right corner, you can find <b>Back</b> button and if you click on this it moves back to the Organizations screen.
1. Once all required fields are filled, click on the <b>Submit</b> button and then it moves to the Organizations screen.
   1. Once the survey is successfully Completed in a Panchayat, a message will be displayed at the top of the screen as <b>"The Organization has been Successfully Updated".</b>
   1. If the survey completion in a Panchayat fails, a message will be displayed at the top of the screen as <b> " Failed to Process the Request ".</b><br>
