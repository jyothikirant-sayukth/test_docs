# Support Executive
Support Executives are customer service representatives, and their primary role is to address user queries and resolve issues efficiently.

## Persona of Support Executive 
1. <b> Qualification :</b> A Graduate with managing customer calls or queries efficiently.
1. Maintaining a positive environment for customers to seek assistance.  
1. Should have a Strong knowledge of product features to address queries effectively. 
1. Should have fluency in English and Hindi for communication. 
1. Should able to work in rotating shifts to provide 24/7 support.

## Responsibilities
1. Responsible for Handling Queries and Concerns.
1. Responsible for Resolving Issues.
1. Responsible for Ticket Workflow Management. 

## How to Login?
1. Enter the [sb.panchayatseva.com](https://app.sb.panchayatseva.com/apu/home) in the Web Browser (Click on the link).
1. Click on the <b>Sign In</b> button located on the top right corner to access the Web Portal Login Screen, where the Support Team will provide the Field Inspector's credentials.
1. Enter the credentials in the [Login Screen](https://app.sb.panchayatseva.com/apu/auth) which are notified to the Support Executive's Mobile Number or Email ID.
   1. At the bottom left corner, you can find <b>Contact Support</b>, and if you click on this, it allows you to contact the Support Team for any login-related issues.
   1. At the bottom right corner, you can find <b>Forgot Password</b>, and if you click on this, it allows you to reset the password.
      1. Enter the <b>SMS OTP</b> and <b>Email OTP</b> that is notified to the registered Mobile number.<br>
{{< image src="images/fp_images/fp_otp.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="200" height="400">}}
1. Enter the Captcha and check the Captcha matches.
{{< notice "note" >}} You need to enter the OTP before it expires.{{< /notice>}}
  1. If the OTP expires, <b> Resend OTP</b> button will be displayed.
      1. Click on <b>Resend OTP</b> button to resend the OTP again.
1. Click on <b>Verify OTP</b> button, it moves to the next screen to set a New Password.<br>
1. Enter the new <b>Password</b>. 
{{< notice "note" >}} Password must have more than 8 characters with a combination of uppercase, lowercase, numbers & special characters *!@#$%^&+=.{{< /notice>}}
1. Re-enter the <b>Password</b> in the Confirm Password field to verify it.
1. Ensure the Password given in the Password & Confirm Password Fields are the same.  
1. After Confirmation of your password, enter the captcha and check that the captcha matches.
1. Click on the <b>Update</b> button to update your new password and you will be moved to the next screen.
1. The Screen displays as <b>Your Password has been updated successfully</b> and <b>Click Here To Login</b> button.<br>
{{< image src="images/fp_images/fp_update_success.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="200" height="400">}}
1. Click the <b>Click Here To Login</b> button to Login again.
{{< notice "note" >}} The screen will proceed to the next step only if the Username entered is correct.{{< /notice >}}
1. Once you log in, you will be redirected to the Support Executive Dashboard screen.<br>

### Creation of a Support Ticket
Support Executives should create support tickets for any issues raised by Users.
{{< notice "note" >}} 
It is Mandatory to create Ticket Category before creating a Support Ticket. 
{{< /notice >}} 
## Navigation 
1. Look for the <b>Support</b> option in the side menu on the left side of the screen, and then find the <b>Support Ticket</b> and by clicking on it will take you to the Support Ticket screen.
### Create 
On the List Screen, click on <b>Create</b> button to move to the Create Screen. 
{{< notice "note" >}} 
Fill all the Mandatory fields (<span style="color: red;">*</span>) on the Create screen. 
{{< /notice >}} 

1. Enter the <b>Title</b> (Mandatory field). 
  > + Allowed Characters: <b>A-Z a-z 0-9</b> 
  > + Minimum Length: <b>6</b> 
  > + Maximum Length: <b>64</b> 

1. Enter the <b>Description</b> (Mandatory field). 
  > + Allowed Characters: <b># A-Z a-z 0-9</b> 
  > + Minimum Length: <b>6</b> 
  > + Maximum Length: <b>256</b> 

1. Choose the appropriate <b>Category</b> from the Dropdown (Mandatory field). 
1. Choose the appropriate <b>Status</b> from the Dropdown (Mandatory field). 
1. Choose the appropriate <b>Priority</b> from the Dropdown (Mandatory field). 
1. Attach the files in the <b>Attachment</b> field (Mandatory field). 
1. Fill the non-mandatory fields : 
  - <b>Docket ID</b> 
  - <b>Mobile Number</b>: Enter the Mobile Number which is registered to the Application and click on <b>validate</b> button to get the details such as Name, Aadhaar, Email, and Designation from the system. 
1. Enter the <b>Captcha</b> displayed on the screen.
1. At the bottom right corner, you can find <b>Back Icon</b> and if you click on this it moves back to the Support Ticket screen.
1. Once all required fields are filled, click the <b>Submit</b> button and then it moves to the Support Ticket Screen.
   1. After successful creation of the Support Ticket, a message will be displayed at the top of the screen as <b> " A Support TicketObj Successfully Updated." </b>
   1. If the Support Ticket creation fails, a message will be displayed at the top of the screen as <b> " Failed to Process the Request ".</b>
### Update 
On the Support Ticket Screen, select the checkbox that you want to update and click the <b>Update</b> button and then it moves to the Update Screen.<br>
   1. If no checkbox is selected, then an Alert Box is displayed as <b>" Please Select one item to Update"</b> and click <b>OK</b> button on the Alert Box to select the checkbox.<br>
1. Modify the fields that you want to edit.
1. Enter the <b>Reason for Updation</b>. 
1. Enter the <b>Captcha</b> displayed on the screen.
1. At the bottom right corner, you can find <b>Back Icon</b> and if you click on this it moves back to the Support Ticket screen.
1. Once all required fields are filled, click the <b>Submit</b> button and then it moves to the Support Ticket screen.
   1. After successful updation of the Support Ticket, a message will be displayed at the top of the screen as <b> " A Support Ticket Successfully Updated." </b>
   1. If the Support Ticket updation fails, a message will be displayed at the top of the screen as <b> " Failed to Process the Request ".</b>

### View 
On the Support Ticket Screen, select the checkbox that you want to view and click the <b>View</b> button and then it moves to the View Screen.
   1. If no checkbox is selected, then an Alert Box is displayed as <b>" Please Select one item to View"</b> and click <b>OK</b> button on the Alert Box to select the checkbox.
{{< notice "note">}} In the View section, the PDF and Excel buttons allow you to download the details of the respective Support Ticket.{{< /notice >}}
1. You can view the selected Support Ticket details.
   1. At the bottom right corner, you can find <b>Back Icon</b> and if you click on this then it moves back to the Support Ticket Screen. 

### Export 
On the Support Ticket  Screen, select the checkboxes that you want to export.
   1. If no checkbox is selected, then an Alert Box is displayed as <b>" Please Select atleast one checkbox to perform export PDF or Excel operation"</b> and click <b>OK</b> button on the Alert Box to select the checkbox.<br>
{{< notice "note">}}In the Export section, you can download details of multiple Support Tickets at once in PDF or Excel format by selecting them.{{< /notice >}}
1. Click the <b>Export</b> button, and then PDF and Excel options will be displayed.
   1. Select <b>PDF</b> Option to export the selected Support Ticket details in PDF format.
   1. Select <b>Excel</b> Option to export the selected Support Ticket details in Excel sheet.

### Notifications Section  
- Receive updates about new or escalated tickets.  
- Notifications appear as a count in the top-right corner.
