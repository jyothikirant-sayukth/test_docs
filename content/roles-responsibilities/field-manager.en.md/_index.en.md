# Field Manager
A Field Manager  is responsible for leading and managing the Field Executive's Work Progress.

## Persona
1. <b>Qualification:</b> A Graduate residing in the native place.
1. Should have strong regional communication skills with professional attire.
   
## Responsibilities
1. Responsible for Tracking the Field Inspector and Field Executive.

## How to Login?
1. Enter the [sb.panchayatseva.com](https://app.sb.panchayatseva.com/apu/home) in the Web Browser (Click on the link).
1. Click on the <b>Sign In</b> button located on the top right corner to access the Web Portal Login Screen, where the Support Team will provide the Field Inspector's credentials.
1. Enter the credentials in the [Login Screen](https://app.sb.panchayatseva.com/apu/auth) which are notified to the Field Manager's Mobile Number or Email ID.
   1. At the bottom left corner, you can find <b>Contact Support</b>, and if you click on this, it allows you to contact the Support Team for any login-related issues.
   1. At the bottom right corner, you can find <b>Forgot Password</b>, and if you click on this, it allows you to reset the password.
      1. Enter the <b>SMS OTP</b> and <b>Email OTP</b> that is notified to the registered Mobile number.<br>
{{< image src="images/fp_images/fp_otp.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="200" height="400">}}
1. Enter the Captcha and check the Captcha matches.
{{< notice "note" >}} You need to enter the OTP before it expires.{{< /notice>}}
  1. If the OTP expires, <b> Resend OTP</b> button will be displayed.
      1. Click on <b>Resend OTP</b> button to resend the OTP again.
1. Click on <b>Verify OTP</b> button, it moves to the next screen to set a New Password.<br>
1. Enter the new <b>Password</b>. 
{{< notice "note" >}} Password must have more than 8 characters with a combination of uppercase, lowercase, numbers & special characters *!@#$%^&+=.{{< /notice>}}
1. Re-enter the <b>Password</b> in the Confirm Password field to verify it.
1. Ensure the Password given in the Password & Confirm Password Fields are the same.  
1. After Confirmation of your password, enter the captcha and check that the captcha matches.
1. Click on the <b>Update</b> button to update your new password and you will be moved to the next screen.
1. The Screen displays as <b>Your Password has been updated successfully</b> and <b>Click Here To Login</b> button.<br>
{{< image src="images/fp_images/fp_update_success.jpg" alt="Alt text" width="200" height="400">}}
1. Click the <b>Click Here To Login</b> button to Login again.
{{< notice "note" >}} The screen will proceed to the next step only if the Username entered is correct.{{< /notice >}}
1. Once you log in, you will be redirected to the Field Manager Dashboard screen.<br>

### GIS Maps 
1. Look for the <b>GIS Maps</b> option in the side menu on the left side of the screen, and then find the <b>Taxable Properties</b> within the GIS Maps; clicking on it will take you to the Property Geo Map screen.
   1. You can view the location of taxable properties, Select the checkbox next to a property name to view its location on the map.
1. Look for the <b>Panchayat Asset</b> within the GIS Maps; clicking on it will take you to the Asset Geo Map screen.
   1. You can view the location of Panchayat Asset, Select the checkbox next to a property name to view its location on the map.
1. Look for the <b>Panchayat Device</b> option in the side menu on the left side of the screen, and then find the <b>Device Incident</b> within the Panchayat Device; clicking on it will take you to the Device Incident Screen.
